    Base

    Modalità Base attiva. Questa unità ti aiuta a parlare di cibo, bevande, spesa e ordini al ristorante in inglese. Imparerai a distinguere tra nomi countable e uncountable, a usare some / any, e a fare richieste semplici in modo naturale. Per la versione estesa usa il toggle BASE / PRO nella barra in alto.

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • parlare di cibo, bevande e acquisti quotidiani
    • distinguere tra nomi countable e uncountable
    • usare some, any e a lot of in frasi semplici
    • chiedere e dire quantità di base
    • ordinare al ristorante o comprare prodotti in un negozio o supermercato

    🧠 Grammar in context

    Countable nouns

    I nomi countable si possono contare.

    • an apple
    • two apples
    • three sandwiches
    • a bottle
    • four eggs

    Possiamo usare:

    • a / an
    • numeri
    • many

    Esempi:

    • I want an apple.
    • We need three tomatoes.
    • There are many shops in this street.

    Uncountable nouns

    I nomi uncountable non si contano normalmente uno per uno.

    • water
    • milk
    • bread
    • rice
    • sugar
    • coffee
    • cheese

    Di solito non usiamo a / an.

    • I want water.
    • We need some rice.
    • There isn't any milk.

    Some / Any

    Usiamo some soprattutto nelle frasi affermative.

    • I need some bread.
    • She bought some apples.

    Usiamo any soprattutto nelle frasi negative e interrogative.

    • We don't have any coffee.
    • Do you have any eggs?

    A lot of

    A lot of si usa sia con countable sia con uncountable.

    • We bought a lot of fruit.
    • There are a lot of customers today.

    Useful question patterns

    • Can I have a coffee, please?
    • How much is it?
    • How many apples do you want?
    • Do you have any water?
    • Would you like anything else?

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Dialogue 1 – at a café

    A: Good afternoon. What would you like? B: Can I have a sandwich and a bottle of water, please? A: Of course. Anything else? B: Yes, a coffee, please. A: Sure. That's £8.

    Dialogue 2 – at the supermarket

    A: Do we have any milk at home? B: No, we don't. We also need some bread and eggs. A: How many eggs do we need? B: A dozen, please.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • I need some bread.
    • We don't have any milk.
    • Can I have a coffee, please?
    • How much is this?
    • I'd like two apples and a bottle of water.

    👉 Ora parla di te:

    • What food do you usually buy?
    • What do you drink in the morning?
    • What do you order in a café?
    • What do you need at home today?

    👉 Fai domande:

    • Do you have any rice?
    • How many tomatoes do we need?
    • Can I pay by card?

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 8–10 frasi su una di queste situazioni:

    1. la tua spesa settimanale
    2. un ordine al bar o al ristorante
    3. cosa c'è e cosa manca nella tua cucina

    Puoi usare questo modello:

    I usually buy some fruit, bread and milk.
    I don't buy much meat.
    At the café, I usually order a coffee and a sandwich.
    At home, we don't have any rice today.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Attenzione a queste parole:

    • bread → /bred/
    • fruit → /fruːt/
    • juice → /dʒuːs/
    • rice → /raɪs/
    • sugar → /ˈʃʊɡə(r)/

    👉 Non leggere tutto come in italiano. In inglese molte parole comuni hanno suoni molto diversi dalla grafia italiana.

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I want a bread
    ✅ I want some bread / I want bread

    ❌ We need a milk
    ✅ We need some milk

    ❌ How much apples?
    ✅ How many apples?

    ❌ I buyed some food
    ✅ I bought some food

    ❌ I want a water
    ✅ I want some water / Can I have a bottle of water?

    🧠 Pattern fondamentali

    • some + plural / uncountable
    • any + plural / uncountable
    • How much + uncountable?
    • How many + plural countable?
    • Can I have ... , please?
    • I'd like ...

    Esempi:

    • I'd like some soup.
    • Do you have any cheese?
    • How many bananas do you want?
    • How much rice do we need?

    ❓ Domande utili

    • What would you like?
    • Can I help you?
    • Do you need anything else?
    • How much is it?
    • How many do you want?
    • Can I pay by card?

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa con some / any / much / many:

    1. We need ______ bread.
    2. Do you have ______ eggs?
    3. How ______ milk do you drink?
    4. How ______ apples do you want?
    5. There aren't ______ customers today.

    Trasforma in domanda:

    1. You have some coffee. → ______
    2. They need three bottles of water. → ______
    3. She wants some cheese. → ______

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Parla per 1 minuto:

    • cosa compri di solito al supermercato
    • cosa c'è nel tuo frigorifero
    • cosa ordini quando esci
    • quali cibi ti piacciono e quali no

    🚀 Mini task

    Simula un dialogo al ristorante.

    Ruolo A: cliente
    Ruolo B: cameriere

    Usa almeno queste espressioni:

    • Can I have ...?
    • I'd like ...
    • Anything else?
    • That's all, thank you.
    • Can I have the bill, please?

    💬 Extra practice

    Rispondi in modo completo:

    • What do you usually buy at the supermarket?
    • Do you drink much coffee?
    • How many meals do you eat a day?
    • Is there any food you never eat?

    🎯 Challenge

    Scrivi un mini-dialogo di 10–12 battute tra un cliente e un commesso oppure tra un cliente e un cameriere. Usa:

    • almeno 4 parole di cibo o bevande
    • some / any
    • una domanda con How much o How many
    • una richiesta educata con Can I have ...? o I'd like ...
