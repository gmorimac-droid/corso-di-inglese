    Base

    Modalità Base attiva. Questa versione presenta le domande fondamentali per chiedere e dare informazioni personali. Per vedere la versione estesa usa il toggle BASE / PRO nella barra in alto.

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • chiedere e dare informazioni personali di base
    • usare le domande con what, where e how old
    • parlare di nome, età, provenienza e lavoro
    • sostenere una mini conversazione iniziale in inglese

    🧠 Grammar in context

    Domande con WH-words

    Le domande personali più comuni iniziano con una parola interrogativa.

    Struttura base:

    WH-word + verb + subject + complement

    What

    Si usa per chiedere il nome, il lavoro o altre informazioni.

    • What is your name?
    • What’s your name?
    • What is your job?
    • What’s your job?

    Where

    Si usa per chiedere la provenienza o il luogo.

    • Where are you from?
    • Where is she from?

    👉 Ricorda l’ordine corretto:

    ❌ Where you are from? ✅ Where are you from?

    How old

    Si usa per chiedere l’età.

    • How old are you?
    • How old is he?

    Risposte:

    • I am 25 years old.
    • I’m 25.
    • She is 19 years old.

    📚 Core vocabulary

    Personal information

    • name
    • surname / last name
    • age
    • job
    • country
    • city
    • phone number
    • email address

    Useful jobs

    • student
    • teacher
    • doctor
    • engineer
    • manager
    • assistant

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Dialogue:

    A: Hi! What’s your name? B: My name is Luca. A: Where are you from? B: I’m from Italy. A: How old are you? B: I’m 28. A: What’s your job? B: I’m an engineer. A: Nice to meet you. B: Nice to meet you too.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti ad alta voce:

    • What’s your name?
    • Where are you from?
    • How old are you?
    • What’s your job?
    • I’m from Italy.
    • I’m 28.

    👉 Ora rispondi con informazioni tue:

    • My name is ______.
    • I’m from ______.
    • I’m ______ years old.
    • I’m a/an ______.

    👉 Poi fai un mini dialogo completo:

    • What’s your name?
    • Where are you from?
    • How old are you?
    • What’s your job?

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi un breve testo di 4–5 frasi.

    Esempio:

    My name is Marco. I’m from Italy. I’m 25 years old. I’m a student. My city is Naples.

    Ora scrivi il tuo testo.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Contrazioni utili

    • What is → What’s
    • I am → I’m
    • She is → She’s

    Focus su years old

    • years old
    • I’m 30 years old.

    👉 Cerca di pronunciare la frase come un unico gruppo ritmico.

    ⚠️ Errori comuni

    ❌ I have 30 years. ✅ I am 30 years old.

    ❌ Where you are from? ✅ Where are you from?

    ❌ What your name is? ✅ What is your name? ✅ What’s your name?

    ❌ I’m engineer. ✅ I’m an engineer.

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa:

    1. What ___ your name?
    2. Where ___ you from?
    3. How old ___ you?
    4. I ___ 25 years old.
    5. What ___ your job?
    6. She ___ from Spain.

    Trasforma in domanda

    1. You are from Italy. → __?
    2. You are 30 years old. → __?
    3. Your name is Anna. → __?
    4. He is a teacher. → __?

    🚀 Mini task

    Simula una conversazione reale di 30–40 secondi. Parla di:

    • name
    • age
    • country
    • city
    • job

    Real conversation starts with real questions. Ask clearly. Answer naturally.
