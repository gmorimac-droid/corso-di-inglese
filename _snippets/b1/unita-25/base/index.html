    Unità 25 – Giving Opinions

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità saprai:

    • esprimere opinioni personali in modo chiaro e naturale
    • dire se sei d’accordo o non sei d’accordo
    • dare una breve motivazione con because
    • usare formule semplici ma efficaci per partecipare a una conversazione
    • evitare errori comuni come I am agree o I think yes

    🧠 Grammar in context

    In inglese esprimere un’opinione è una competenza fondamentale, soprattutto a livello B1, perché permette di partecipare davvero a una conversazione.

    Le strutture più comuni sono:

    • I think...
    • I don’t think...
    • I believe...
    • In my opinion...

    Esempi:

    • I think this idea is useful.
    • I don’t think this plan will work.
    • I believe English is essential for work.
    • In my opinion, this is the best solution.

    1. Expressing opinions

    Formule base

    • I think this is a good idea.
    • I think the film is too long.
    • I don’t think this restaurant is very good.
    • In my opinion, public transport should be cheaper.

    Perché sono importanti

    Queste strutture ti aiutano a:

    • iniziare una conversazione
    • rispondere a una domanda personale
    • commentare un’idea, una notizia, un film, una decisione
    • esprimere accordo o disaccordo in modo educato

    2. Agreeing and disagreeing

    Agreement

    • I agree.
    • I agree with you.
    • You’re right.
    • I think so.
    • Exactly.

    Disagreement

    • I don’t agree.
    • I don’t think so.
    • I’m not sure.
    • I see your point, but...
    • I think you’re wrong. (più diretto)

    3. Giving a reason

    Per rendere la tua opinione più naturale, aggiungi una motivazione con because.

    • I think this app is useful because it saves time.
    • I don’t think it’s a good idea because it’s too expensive.
    • In my opinion, online classes are effective because they are flexible.

    📚 Useful language

    Opinion expressions

    • I think...
    • I don’t think...
    • In my opinion...
    • I believe...
    • For me...

    Reaction expressions

    • Really?
    • That’s true.
    • Maybe.
    • I’m not sure.
    • It depends.

    🎧 Reading / Dialogue

    Dialogue 1

    A: What do you think of remote work? B: I think it’s great because it saves time. A: I agree, but I think people also need face-to-face contact. B: Yes, that’s true.

    Dialogue 2

    A: In my opinion, travelling by train is better than flying. B: I’m not sure. I think flying is faster. A: That’s true, but trains are usually more comfortable.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • I think this is a good idea.
    • I don’t think it will work.
    • I agree with you.
    • I don’t think so.
    • In my opinion, this is the best option.

    Ora completa a voce:

    • I think English is important because ...
    • In my opinion, social media is ...
    • I don’t think ... because ...
    • I agree that ...
    • I’m not sure if ...

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 8 frasi per esprimere opinioni su questi temi:

    • work
    • study
    • technology
    • travel
    • food
    • free time

    Usa almeno:

    • 3 frasi con I think...
    • 2 con I don’t think...
    • 1 con In my opinion...
    • 2 con because

    Esempio:

    I think learning English is important because it opens many opportunities.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Attenzione a queste espressioni:

    • I think → /aɪ θɪŋk/
    • I agree → /aɪ əˈɡriː/
    • I don’t think so → ritmo naturale e collegato

    Allenati a dire:

    • I think it’s useful.
    • I don’t think so.
    • I agree with you.
    • In my opinion, it’s better.

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I am agree
    ✅ I agree

    ❌ I think yes
    ✅ I think so

    ❌ I don’t agree with this idea because is expensive
    ✅ I don’t agree with this idea because it is expensive

    ❌ In my opinion is better
    ✅ In my opinion, it is better

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa:

    1. I ___ this is a good idea.
    2. I don’t ___ so.
    3. I ___ with you.
    4. In my ___, this is better.
    5. I think it’s useful ___ it saves time.

    🔄 Transform

    Trasforma le frasi usando un’espressione di opinione:

    1. This plan is good. → I think ______
    2. This course is useful. → In my opinion, ______
    3. It is not a good idea. → I don’t think ______
    4. Online meetings are practical. → I believe ______

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Rispondi oralmente:

    • What do you think of studying online?
    • Do you think technology helps people?
    • Do you agree that English is necessary for work?
    • What is your opinion about living in a big city?

    🚀 Mini task

    Parla per 2 minuti su questo tema:

    “What makes a good place to live?”

    Prova a includere:

    • 3 opinioni
    • 2 ragioni con because
    • 1 punto di accordo o disaccordo

    English becomes more real when you can explain what you think and why you think it.
