    Base

    Modalità Base attiva. Questa unità ti aiuta a parlare del futuro in inglese usando will e be going to. Imparerai a distinguere tra decisioni spontanee, piani già decisi e semplici frasi sul futuro, evitando errori molto comuni. Per la versione estesa usa il toggle BASE / PRO nella barra in alto.

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • parlare di piani futuri con be going to
    • esprimere decisioni prese sul momento con will
    • fare domande e frasi negative sul futuro
    • usare espressioni di tempo come tomorrow, next week, tonight, later
    • evitare errori comuni come I will to go o I going to study

    🧠 Grammar in context

    Be going to → piani e intenzioni

    Usiamo be going to quando parliamo di qualcosa che abbiamo già deciso o che intendiamo fare.

    • I am going to study tonight.
    • She is going to visit her grandparents.
    • We are going to travel next month.

    Schema:

    • I am going to + verbo
    • He / She is going to + verbo
    • We / They are going to + verbo

    Esempi:

    • I am going to cook dinner.
    • They are going to watch a film.
    • He is going to start a new course.

    Will → decisioni spontanee

    Usiamo will quando decidiamo qualcosa nel momento in cui parliamo.

    • I will help you.
    • OK, I will call her now.
    • Don't worry, we will wait.

    Schema:

    • I / You / He / She / We / They will + verbo

    Forma contratta:

    • I'll help.
    • You'll see.
    • We'll go later.

    Negative and questions

    Going to

    • I am not going to work tomorrow.
    • She isn't going to come.
    • Are you going to study tonight?
    • Is he going to buy a new phone?

    Will

    • I will not call him now.
    • She won't be late.
    • Will you help me?
    • Will they come with us?

    Differenza base

    Struttura Uso principale
    be going to piano / intenzione già decisa
    will decisione presa sul momento

    Esempi:

    • I am going to visit Rome next weekend. → l'ho già deciso
    • OK, I will come with you. → lo decido adesso

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Dialogue 1 – making plans

    A: What are you going to do this weekend? B: I am going to visit my sister. A: Nice. Are you going to stay there all weekend? B: No, just for one day.

    Dialogue 2 – spontaneous decision

    A: I can't open this box. B: Wait, I will help you. A: Thanks. B: No problem.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • I am going to study tonight.
    • We are going to travel next month.
    • I will help you.
    • I will call you later.
    • Are you going to come?
    • Will you be at home tonight?

    👉 Ora parla di te:

    • What are you going to do tomorrow?
    • Are you going to study this evening?
    • What are you going to do next weekend?
    • When will you call a friend or a family member?

    👉 Fai domande:

    • Are you going to work tomorrow?
    • Will you help me?
    • Are they going to travel this summer?
    • Will she call later?

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 8 frasi:

    • 4 con be going to
    • 4 con will

    Puoi seguire questo modello:

    I am going to study English tonight.
    We are going to visit our friends on Saturday.
    She is going to buy a new bag next week.
    They are going to stay at home tomorrow.

    I will help you with your homework.
    I will call you later.
    We will wait here.
    He will open the window.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Attenzione a queste forme naturali:

    • going to → spesso si sente gonna nel parlato informale
    • I will → I'll
    • we will → we'll
    • will not → won't

    Esempi:

    • I'm going to go. → nel parlato informale può suonare come I'm gonna go
    • I'll call you later.
    • We won't be late.

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I will to go
    ✅ I will go

    ❌ I going to study
    ✅ I am going to study

    ❌ I will going
    ✅ I will go

    ❌ She going to work tomorrow
    ✅ She is going to work tomorrow

    ❌ Are you going study tonight?
    ✅ Are you going to study tonight?

    🧠 Pattern fondamentali

    • be going to + verbo base
    • will + verbo base
    • be cambia in base al soggetto
    • dopo will non usiamo mai to

    Esempi:

    • I am going to study.
    • He is going to work.
    • They are going to travel.
    • I will help.
    • We will call later.

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa con am / is / are / will:

    1. I ___ going to study tonight.
    2. She ___ going to call later.
    3. We ___ help you.
    4. They ___ going to travel next month.
    5. I ___ open the door.
    6. He ___ going to stay at home.

    🔄 Trasforma

    Trasforma le frasi:

    1. I study tonight. → I am going to ______
    2. I help you. → I will ______
    3. She visit her aunt next week. → She is going to ______
    4. We call you later. → We will ______
    5. They stay at home tomorrow. → They are going to ______

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Parla per 1 minuto di:

    • i tuoi piani per domani
    • cosa farai nel weekend
    • una decisione che puoi prendere sul momento oggi

    🚀 Mini task

    Scrivi o racconta un breve testo su:

    My plans for next week

    Prova a usare almeno:

    • 3 frasi con going to
    • 2 frasi con will
    • 4 espressioni di tempo futuro
