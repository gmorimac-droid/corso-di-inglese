    Unità 02 – Verb "To Be"

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • usare correttamente il verbo to be
    • fare frasi affermative, negative e domande
    • dare informazioni semplici su persone
    • rispondere a domande base

    🧠 Grammar in context

    Forma affermativa

    Subject Verb
    I am
    You are
    He is
    She is
    It is
    We are
    They are

    Esempi

    • I am a student
    • You are my friend
    • She is from Italy
    • They are teachers

    Forma negativa

    Struttura:

    👉 Subject + verb + NOT

    • I am not (I’m not)
    • You are not (You aren’t)
    • He is not (He isn’t)

    Esempi

    • I am not a teacher
    • She is not from Spain
    • They are not at home

    Forma interrogativa

    Struttura:

    👉 Verb + subject

    • Am I...?
    • Are you...?
    • Is he...?

    Esempi

    • Are you a student?
    • Is she Italian?
    • Are they friends?

    Risposte brevi

    • Yes, I am / No, I’m not
    • Yes, he is / No, he isn’t
    • Yes, they are / No, they aren’t

    📚 Core vocabulary

    Jobs

    • student
    • teacher
    • engineer
    • doctor
    • worker

    Countries

    • Italy
    • Spain
    • France
    • Germany
    • USA

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Dialogue:

    A: Are you a student?
    B: Yes, I am.
    A: Are you from Italy?
    B: Yes, I am. What about you?
    A: I’m from Spain.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • I am a student
    • I am from Italy
    • She is a teacher
    • They are friends

    👉 Rispondi:

    • Are you a student?
    • Are you from Italy?
    • Are you a teacher?

    👉 Ora parla di te:

    • I am ___
    • I am from ___
    • I am a ___

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 5 frasi:

    1. I am ______
    2. I am from ______
    3. I am not ______
    4. She is ______
    5. They are ______

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Contrazioni molto importanti:

    • I am → I’m
    • You are → You’re
    • He is → He’s
    • She is → She’s
    • They are → They’re

    👉 Parla in modo naturale:

    ❌ I am Marco
    ✅ I’m Marco

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I have 25 years
    ✅ I am 25 years old

    ❌ I am agree
    ✅ I agree

    👉 In inglese to be è usato in modo diverso rispetto all’italiano.

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa:

    1. She ___ from Italy
    2. They ___ students
    3. I ___ not a teacher
    4. ___ you from Spain?
    5. ___ he a doctor?

    🚀 Mini task

    Fai una mini presentazione:

    • Name
    • Country
    • Job

    👉 Esempio:

    Hi, I’m Marco. I’m from Italy. I’m a student.

    💬 Extra practice (consigliato)

    Fai queste domande a qualcuno (o immagina le risposte):

    • Are you a student?
    • Where are you from?
    • Are you a teacher?

    Practice simple sentences every day.
    Fluency starts with repetition.
