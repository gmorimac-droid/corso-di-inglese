    Unità 27 – Modals (must, should, have to)

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • dare consigli
    • esprimere obblighi e necessità
    • parlare di regole
    • usare must, should e have to correttamente

    🧠 Grammar in context

    Should → consigli

    👉 per dire cosa è una buona idea

    • You should study more
    • You should sleep early

    Must → obbligo forte / regole

    👉 forte, spesso personale

    • You must stop
    • You must study

    Have to → obbligo reale

    👉 regole esterne / necessità

    • I have to work
    • I have to wake up early

    ⚖️ Differenza chiave

    Modal Uso
    should consiglio
    must obbligo forte
    have to obbligo reale

    📚 Esempi reali

    • You should study English every day
    • You must wear a seatbelt
    • I have to work tomorrow

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Dialogue:

    A: I’m tired.
    B: You should sleep more.

    A: I have an exam.
    B: You must study.

    A: I have a job.
    B: I have to work every day.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • You should study
    • You must stop
    • I have to work

    👉 Ora prova:

    • You should ___
    • You must ___
    • I have to ___

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 6 frasi:

    👉 2 consigli
    👉 2 obblighi
    👉 2 necessità

    Esempio:

    You should study more.
    You should eat healthy food.
    I must finish this work.
    You must stop here.
    I have to wake up early.
    I have to go to work.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Attenzione:

    • should → /ʃʊd/
    • must → /mʌst/

    👉 suoni brevi e veloci

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I must to go
    ✅ I must go

    ❌ I have to go
    ✅ (corretto)

    ❌ You should to study
    ✅ You should study

    👉 dopo modali → verbo base (no “to”)

    🧠 Pattern fondamentale

    • Subject + modal + verb

    👉 sempre senza “to”

    🔁 Negativo

    • You shouldn’t smoke
    • You mustn’t do that
    • I don’t have to work today

    📚 Differenza negativa (importante)

    • mustn’t → proibizione
    • don’t have to → non necessario

    Esempi

    • You mustn’t smoke → vietato
    • You don’t have to work → non serve

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa:

    1. You ___ study more
    2. I ___ work tomorrow
    3. You ___ stop here
    4. You ___ smoke (consiglio negativo)

    🔄 Trasforma

    Completa:

    1. I go → I must ______
    2. I work → I have to ______
    3. You study → You should ______

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Parla:

    👉 consigli per:

    • studiare
    • vivere meglio
    • lavorare

    🚀 Mini task

    Parla per 2 minuti:

    👉 dai 5 consigli a una persona

    💬 Extra practice

    Rispondi:

    • What should you do to learn English?
    • What do you have to do every day?
    • What must you do at work?

    🎯 Challenge

    Crea:

    • 3 consigli
    • 2 obblighi
    • 2 proibizioni

    🧩 Bonus (B1+)

    Usa:

    • You should try...
    • You shouldn’t...

    English becomes powerful when you can give advice and rules.
