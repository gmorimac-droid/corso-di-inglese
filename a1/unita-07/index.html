    Unità 07 – There is / There are

    Modalità Base attiva. Questa versione ti aiuta a usare there is / there are per descrivere una stanza, un ufficio o un altro luogo in modo semplice. Per vedere la versione estesa usa il toggle BASE / PRO nella barra in alto.

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • dire cosa c’è o cosa non c’è in un luogo
    • usare there is con il singolare
    • usare there are con il plurale
    • descrivere una stanza con frasi semplici e corrette

    🧠 Grammar in context

    There is

    Usiamo there is per dire che c’è una sola cosa.

    • There is a table.
    • There is a bed in the room.
    • There is a lamp on the desk.

    There are

    Usiamo there are per dire che ci sono più cose.

    • There are two chairs.
    • There are three books on the table.
    • There are two windows in the room.

    Schema rapido

    Singular Plural
    There is There are

    Forme negative

    • There isn’t a TV.
    • There aren’t any plants.

    Domande utili

    • Is there a bathroom?
    • Is there a desk?
    • Are there two chairs?
    • Are there any books?

    Risposte brevi:

    • Yes, there is.
    • No, there isn’t.
    • Yes, there are.
    • No, there aren’t.

    📚 Core vocabulary

    In a room

    • room
    • table
    • chair
    • bed
    • desk
    • lamp
    • sofa
    • shelf
    • door
    • window
    • wall
    • picture

    In an office or study area

    • computer
    • laptop
    • screen
    • keyboard
    • mouse
    • printer
    • book
    • notebook
    • pen
    • bag

    Position words

    • in
    • on
    • under
    • next to
    • near

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Text:

    There is a desk in my room.
    There is a lamp on the desk.
    There are two books next to the lamp.
    There is a chair under the window.
    There are three pictures on the wall.
    There isn’t a TV in the room.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • There is a table in the room.
    • There is a lamp on the desk.
    • There are two chairs.
    • There are three books on the shelf.
    • There isn’t a sofa.
    • Are there any windows?

    Ora completa con informazioni vere:

    • There is a ______ in my room.
    • There are ______ on my desk.
    • There isn’t a ______ in the room.
    • There are ______ windows.

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 6 frasi per descrivere una stanza.

    Guida:

    1. a bed / a desk / a table
    2. one lamp
    3. two or three books
    4. one window
    5. something that is not in the room
    6. one object with on / under / next to

    Esempio:

    There is a bed in the room.
    There is a desk near the window.
    There is a lamp on the desk.
    There are two books on the desk.
    There is a chair next to the desk.
    There isn’t a TV.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Forma contratta

    Nel parlato naturale:

    • There is → There’s

    Esempi:

    • There’s a chair.
    • There’s a lamp on the table.

    Con il plurale di solito manteniamo chiaramente:

    • There are two books.
    • There are three windows.

    ⚠️ Errori comuni

    ❌ There are a chair.
    ✅ There is a chair.

    ❌ There is two books.
    ✅ There are two books.

    ❌ Is a desk in the room.
    ✅ There is a desk in the room.

    ❌ There have two windows.
    ✅ There are two windows.

    👉 Controlla sempre se il nome è singolare o plurale.

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa

    1. There ___ a bed in the room.
    2. There ___ two chairs.
    3. There ___ a lamp on the desk.
    4. There ___ three books on the shelf.
    5. There ___ not a TV.
    6. ___ there any windows?

    Trasforma

    1. There is a chair. → (plurale) ______
    2. There are two books. → (singolare) ______
    3. There is a lamp. → (negativa) ______

    🚀 Mini task

    Descrivi la tua stanza, il tuo ufficio o la tua cucina usando:

    • almeno 5 oggetti
    • almeno 2 frasi con there is
    • almeno 2 frasi con there are
    • almeno 1 frase negativa

    Modalità Professional attiva. Questa versione amplia la lezione su there is / there are con descrizioni più naturali di ambienti reali, uso di a / an / some / any, posizione degli oggetti, forme interrogative e negative, errori tipici degli italofoni, speaking drills, writing guidato e self-check finale.

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • descrivere stanze, uffici e spazi quotidiani in modo chiaro e credibile
    • usare correttamente there is / there are al singolare e al plurale
    • costruire frasi negative e domande naturali
    • combinare there is / there are con a, an, some, any e con semplici preposizioni di luogo
    • evitare gli errori più comuni degli italofoni quando descrivono ciò che vedono intorno a sé

    🧭 Focus della lezione

    Questa unità non serve solo a dire c’è / ci sono. Serve a fare qualcosa di molto più utile: descrivere uno spazio in modo organizzato.

    In inglese iniziale, saper descrivere una stanza, una casa, una scrivania o un ufficio ti permette di:

    1. spiegare dove ti trovi
    2. parlare di oggetti presenti o assenti
    3. rispondere a domande pratiche
    4. fare una descrizione visiva semplice ma efficace
    5. prepararti alle unità successive su preposizioni, casa, città e descrizione di luoghi

    L’obiettivo non è creare frasi isolate, ma produrre una mini-descrizione coerente.

    🧠 Grammar in context

    There is / There are

    Usiamo there is / there are per introdurre l’esistenza di qualcosa in un luogo.

    Singular: there is

    Per una cosa sola:

    • There is a desk in the room.
    • There is an old sofa near the wall.
    • There is a lamp on the table.

    Plural: there are

    Per più cose:

    • There are two chairs in the office.
    • There are some books on the shelf.
    • There are three windows in the living room.

    Il punto essenziale è questo:

    • there is → una cosa
    • there are → più cose

    A / An / Some / Any

    Quando descrivi un luogo, molto spesso queste parole lavorano insieme a there is / there are.

    a / an

    Per un nome singolare numerabile:

    • There is a chair.
    • There is a table.
    • There is an armchair.
    • There is an office next door.

    some

    Molto comune nelle frasi affermative plurali:

    • There are some books on the desk.
    • There are some files in the drawer.

    any

    Molto comune nelle domande e nelle frasi negative:

    • Are there any chairs in the room?
    • There aren’t any pictures on the wall.

    👉 A questo livello va benissimo ricordare questa regola pratica:

    • some → spesso affermativa
    • any → spesso domanda / negativa

    Negative forms

    Singular

    • There isn’t a TV in the room.
    • There isn’t a window in this office.

    Plural

    • There aren’t any chairs here.
    • There aren’t any books on the shelf.

    Quando parli di ciò che manca, queste forme sono essenziali.

    Questions

    Singular

    • Is there a bathroom?
    • Is there a printer in the office?
    • Is there a lamp on the desk?

    Plural

    • Are there any windows?
    • Are there two meeting rooms?
    • Are there any people in the office?

    Short answers

    • Yes, there is.
    • No, there isn’t.
    • Yes, there are.
    • No, there aren’t.

    There is / It is

    Questa distinzione è molto importante.

    There is / There are

    Si usa per introdurre qualcosa:

    • There is a laptop on the desk.
    • There are two windows in the room.

    It is / They are

    Si usa quando l’oggetto è già noto e vuoi aggiungere informazioni:

    • There is a laptop on the desk. It is new.
    • There are two windows in the room. They are very large.

    👉 Quindi:

    • there is / there are = esistenza
    • it is / they are = descrizione dell’oggetto già introdotto

    Prepositions of place you need now

    Per rendere la descrizione più utile, aggiungi semplici preposizioni di luogo:

    • in the room
    • on the desk
    • under the table
    • next to the bed
    • near the window
    • behind the chair
    • in front of the sofa

    Esempi:

    • There is a lamp on the desk.
    • There are two boxes under the table.
    • There is a chair next to the window.

    Furniture and room items

    • armchair
    • shelf
    • lamp
    • carpet
    • wardrobe
    • drawer
    • mirror

    • plant

    Office and study objects

    • laptop

    • screen

    • mouse

    • file

    🧩 Funzioni comunicative

    Con questa unità impari a:

    1. Descrivere una stanza

    • There is a bed next to the wall.
    • There are two shelves above the desk.

    2. Parlare di ciò che manca

    • There isn’t a sofa in the room.
    • There aren’t any pictures on the wall.

    3. Fare domande pratiche

    • Is there a bathroom?
    • Are there any plugs near the desk?

    4. Guidare visivamente qualcuno

    • There is a printer on the left.
    • There are some files under the desk.

    5. Fare una mini-presentazione di casa o ufficio

    • In my office, there is a large desk and there are two screens.

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Text 1 – Describing a bedroom

    There is a bed in the middle of the room.
    There is a small desk near the window.
    There is a lamp on the desk and there are some books under it.
    There are two pictures on the wall.
    There isn’t a TV, but there is a laptop on the chair.

    Text 2 – Describing an office

    In our office, there are six desks and twelve chairs.
    There is a printer near the door.
    There are some documents on the main table.
    There isn’t a meeting room on this floor, but there are two phone booths.
    There are also a few plants near the windows.

    Notice

    Osserva che le descrizioni più naturali non sono una lista casuale. Spesso seguono un ordine:

    1. visione generale del luogo
    2. oggetti principali
    3. oggetti secondari
    4. elementi mancanti
    5. dettagli finali

    🗣️ Speaking

    Drill 1 – Singular or plural?

    Completa oralmente:

    • There is a ______ in the room.
    • There are two ______ near the wall.
    • There isn’t a ______ on the table.
    • There aren’t any ______ under the desk.

    Drill 2 – Add position

    Espandi le frasi:

    • There is a lamp. → There is a lamp on the desk.
    • There are two chairs. → There are two chairs near the window.
    • There is a bag. → There is a bag under the table.

    Drill 3 – Ask and answer

    Pratica in coppia o da solo:

    • Is there a printer in the office?
    • Are there any books on the shelf?
    • Is there a sofa in the living room?
    • Are there two doors in the room?

    Rispondi con short answers e poi con una frase completa.

    Drill 4 – One-minute room description

    Parla per 1 minuto e prova a usare:

    • 3 frasi con there is
    • 3 frasi con there are
    • 1 frase negativa
    • 2 preposizioni di luogo

    ✍️ Writing

    Task 1 – Guided description

    Scrivi 8–10 frasi su una stanza reale o immaginaria.

    Checklist:

    • una frase introduttiva generale
    • almeno 3 oggetti singolari
    • almeno 3 oggetti plurali
    • almeno 2 dettagli con preposizioni di luogo
    • almeno 1 frase negativa

    Modello:

    In my room, there is a desk near the window.
    There is a lamp on the desk.
    There are some books next to the lamp.
    There is a chair under the window.
    There are two pictures on the wall.
    There isn’t a TV.
    There is a small plant on the shelf.

    Task 2 – Compare two spaces

    Scrivi 4 frasi su una stanza a casa e 4 frasi su un ufficio o aula.

    Obiettivo: imparare a riusare la stessa struttura in contesti diversi.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    There is → There’s

    Nel parlato naturale la forma contratta è molto comune:

    • There’s a chair by the door.
    • There’s a lamp on the desk.

    There are

    Non sempre si contrae nel parlato standard lento. All’inizio è meglio pronunciarlo bene:

    • There are two books.
    • There are three people in the office.

    Rhythm tip

    Le parole forti di solito sono i nomi e le posizioni:

    • There’s a lamp on the desk.
    • There are two chairs near the window.

    ⚠️ Errori comuni degli italiani

    1. Usare there have

    ❌ There have two chairs.
    ✅ There are two chairs.

    2. Confondere singolare e plurale

    ❌ There is two windows.
    ✅ There are two windows.

    ❌ There are a desk.
    ✅ There is a desk.

    3. Dimenticare there

    ❌ Is a lamp on the table.
    ✅ There is a lamp on the table.

    4. Usare male any

    ❌ There aren’t some books.
    ✅ There aren’t any books.

    5. Usare is con liste plurali

    ❌ There is a desk and two chairs.
    ✅ There are two chairs and a desk.
    ✅ There is a desk and there are two chairs.

    👉 In inglese reale, quando la frase diventa lunga, spesso è più chiaro dividerla in due.

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa

    1. There ___ a chair next to the desk.
    2. There ___ three books on the table.
    3. There ___ not a printer in this room.
    4. Are there ___ windows in the office?
    5. There are ___ files in the drawer.
    6. Is there ___ lamp near the bed?

    Correggi gli errori

    1. There is two computers.
    2. There are a chair near the window.
    3. There isn’t some books on the shelf.
    4. There have a printer in the office.
    5. Is there any chairs?

    Trasforma

    1. There is a lamp on the desk. → negativa
    2. There are some chairs in the room. → domanda
    3. There is a window near the bed. → plurale
    4. There aren’t any pictures on the wall. → affermativa

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Rispondi oralmente:

    • Is there a desk in your room?
    • Are there any books near your bed?
    • Is there a TV in your kitchen?
    • Are there plants in your office or study area?
    • What is on your desk right now?

    Poi prova a unire le risposte in una mini descrizione continua.

    🚀 Mini task

    Immagina di mostrare la tua stanza o il tuo ufficio a una persona straniera in videochiamata.

    Parla per 60–90 secondi e includi:

    • descrizione generale del luogo
    • oggetti principali
    • posizione di almeno 3 oggetti
    • una cosa che manca
    • una frase finale con la tua opinione

    Esempio finale:

    My room is small but comfortable. There is a desk near the window and there is a lamp on it. There are some books under the desk and there are two pictures on the wall. There isn’t a TV, but there is a laptop on the chair. I like this room because it is simple and quiet.
