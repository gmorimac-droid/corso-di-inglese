    Unità 45 – Reported Speech (Advanced Use)

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • trasformare il discorso diretto in indiretto
    • usare reported speech in modo naturale
    • adattare tempi, pronomi e riferimenti
    • parlare e scrivere in modo più fluido e professionale

    🧠 Il salto da B2 a C1

    👉 B2:

    • trasformi frasi

    👉 C1:

    • racconti informazioni

    👉 non stai più traducendo
    👉 stai riportando idee

    🧩 Direct vs Reported Speech

    Direct Speech

    👉 parole esatte

    • He said: “I am tired”

    Reported Speech

    👉 parole riportate

    • He said that he was tired

    🔁 Backshifting (regola base)

    👉 quando riporti nel passato:

    Direct Reported
    present past
    past past perfect
    will would

    📚 Esempi

    • “I am tired” → He said he was tired
    • “I worked” → He said he had worked
    • “I will go” → He said he would go

    🧠 Cambiamenti importanti

    Pronomi

    • I → he / she
    • my → his / her

    Tempo

    • now → then
    • today → that day
    • yesterday → the day before
    • tomorrow → the next day

    Esempio completo

    “I am working today”

    👉 He said that he was working that day

    🔥 Livello C1: Uso naturale

    👉 NON sempre fai backshift

    Quando NON cambia

    👉 se è ancora vero

    • “I live in Rome”
      → He said he lives in Rome

    🧠 Reporting verbs (C1)

    👉 non usare solo “say”

    Verbi comuni

    Verbo Uso
    say dire
    tell dire a qualcuno
    explain spiegare
    suggest suggerire
    admit ammettere
    promise promettere

    Esempi

    • He explained that he was late
    • She admitted that she had made a mistake
    • They promised they would help

    🧩 Reporting questions

    Yes / No questions

    👉 if / whether

    • “Are you coming?”
      → He asked if I was coming

    Wh- questions

    👉 mantieni la parola interrogativa

    • “Where do you live?”
      → He asked where I lived

    🧠 Reporting commands

    Struttura

    tell / ask + object + to + verb

    Esempi

    • “Close the door”
      → He told me to close the door
    • “Don’t be late”
      → He told me not to be late

    🔁 Advanced structures (C1)

    Suggest / recommend

    👉 struttura:

    suggest + verb-ing

    • He suggested going earlier

    Offer / promise

    • He offered to help
    • She promised to call

    🎧 Reading

    She explained that she had been working on the project for months and that she was planning to finish it soon. She also mentioned that she might need some help.

    🗣️ Speaking

    👉 usa reported speech per raccontare:

    • conversazioni
    • riunioni
    • esperienze

    Esempio

    He told me that the project was difficult and that we needed more time.

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi un testo (10–12 frasi):

    👉 racconta:

    • una conversazione
    • una riunione
    • qualcosa che qualcuno ha detto

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (C1)

    Errore Corretto
    He said me He told me
    He said me that He told me that
    He said that I am tired He said that I was tired

    🧠 Pattern avanzati

    👉 combinare più informazioni:

    • He said that he was tired and that he needed a break

    🔁 Upgrade (C1 reale)

    👉 usa più reporting verbs:

    Esempio

    He explained that the situation was complicated, and he suggested waiting before making a decision.

    🧠 Strategia mentale

    👉 non tradurre

    👉 pensa:

    • cosa ha detto?
    • cosa è importante?

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa:

    1. “I am tired” → He said he ___ tired
    2. “I will go” → He said he ___ go
    3. “Where do you live?” → He asked where I ___
    4. “Close the door” → He told me ___

    🔄 Trasforma

    1. “I am working” → ______
    2. “I went home” → ______
    3. “I will call you” → ______

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Parla:

    👉 racconta:

    • una conversazione recente
    • cosa qualcuno ti ha detto

    🚀 Mini task

    Parla per 4 minuti:

    👉 racconta una storia usando reported speech

    💬 Extra practice

    Rispondi:

    • What did your friend tell you recently?
    • What did your boss say?
    • What did you learn from others?

    🎯 Challenge (C1)

    Racconta una conversazione complessa:

    👉 usando almeno 5 reporting verbs

    🧩 Bonus (C1+)

    Usa:

    • He mentioned that...
    • She pointed out that...

    🔥 Insight finale

    👉 B1: ripeti
    👉 B2: trasformi
    👉 C1: racconti

    Advanced English is not about repeating words.
    It is about transmitting meaning.
