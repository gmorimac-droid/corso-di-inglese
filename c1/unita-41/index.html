    Unità 41 – Advanced Tenses (Nuance & Control)

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • usare i tempi verbali con precisione avanzata
    • capire le differenze sottili tra tempi simili
    • scegliere il tempo giusto in base al significato
    • parlare e scrivere con maggiore controllo

    🧠 Il salto da B2 a C1

    👉 B2:

    • uso corretto dei tempi

    👉 C1:

    • uso preciso dei tempi

    👉 non è più:

    ✔️ “è giusto o sbagliato”

    👉 ma:

    ✔️ “qual è più naturale?”
    ✔️ “qual è più preciso?”

    ⚖️ Present Perfect vs Past Simple (Nuance)

    Base (già conosciuta)

    • I went → tempo preciso
    • I have gone → esperienza / risultato

    Livello C1 (sfumatura)

    👉 confronto:

    • I lived in London for 2 years
      👉 esperienza finita

    • I have lived in London for 2 years
      👉 esperienza collegata al presente

    Differenza reale

    Frase Significato
    I lived there non vivo più lì
    I have lived there potrebbe essere ancora vero

    🧠 Present Perfect Continuous vs Simple

    👉 confronto avanzato:

    • I have worked all day
    • I have been working all day

    Differenza

    Forma Focus
    Present Perfect risultato
    Present Perfect Continuous durata / processo

    Esempi

    • I have written 5 emails
      👉 risultato

    • I have been writing emails all morning
      👉 attività continua

    🔁 Past Continuous vs Past Simple

    👉 non solo “azione in corso”

    Esempio avanzato

    • I was working when he called
      👉 contesto

    • I worked when he called
      👉 sequenza (meno naturale)

    Uso C1

    👉 Past Continuous = background

    Esempio

    I was walking through the city when I saw something strange.

    🔮 Future Nuance (C1)

    👉 confronto:

    • I will do it
    • I’m going to do it
    • I’m doing it

    Differenze reali

    Forma Uso
    will decisione / promessa
    going to intenzione
    present continuous piano organizzato

    Livello C1

    👉 scegliere in base al contesto:

    • I’ll call you (decisione ora)
    • I’m going to call you (intenzione)
    • I’m calling you at 8 (programma)

    🧠 Time Perspective (C1 Skill)

    👉 pensa sempre:

    • quando succede?
    • è finito?
    • è ancora rilevante?

    🧩 Esempio avanzato

    I have been working here for 3 years, and I really enjoy it. Before that, I worked in a different company.

    👉 perfetto mix tempi

    🎧 Reading

    I have been studying English for several years, and I have recently started to feel more confident. Before that, I struggled a lot and I didn’t understand native speakers.

    🗣️ Speaking

    👉 usa combinazioni:

    • I have been...
    • I have...
    • I used to...

    👉 esempio:

    I have been learning English for a long time, but I have improved a lot recently.

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi un testo (8–12 frasi):

    👉 includi:

    • presente
    • passato
    • esperienza
    • cambiamento

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (livello avanzato)

    Errore Corretto
    I am living here since 2 years I have been living here for 2 years
    I have worked yesterday I worked yesterday
    I work here since 2020 I have worked here since 2020

    🧠 Pattern avanzati

    👉 combinare tempi:

    • I have been working here and I really enjoy it
    • I worked there before I moved here

    🔁 Upgrade (C1 reale)

    👉 usa più tempi nella stessa frase

    Esempio

    I have been working on this project for weeks, and I finally finished it yesterday.

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa:

    1. I ___ (live) here since 2020
    2. I ___ (work) yesterday
    3. I have been ___ (study) all day
    4. Before that, I ___ (work) in another company

    🔄 Trasforma

    1. I live here (since 2020) → ______
    2. I study all day → ______
    3. I finished → aggiungi contesto

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Parla:

    👉 il tuo percorso:

    • prima
    • adesso
    • cambiamento

    🚀 Mini task

    Parla per 3–4 minuti:

    👉 usa almeno 3 tempi diversi

    💬 Extra practice

    Rispondi:

    • How has your English improved?
    • What did you do before?
    • What are you doing now?

    🎯 Challenge (C1)

    Crea un discorso con:

    • present perfect
    • past simple
    • present continuous

    👉 senza fermarti

    🧩 Bonus (C1+)

    Usa:

    • I have recently...
    • I have been...
    • Before that...

    🔥 Insight finale

    👉 A1–B2 = imparare i tempi
    👉 C1 = controllare i tempi

    At an advanced level, grammar is not about rules.
    It is about choices.
