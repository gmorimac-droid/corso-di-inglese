    Unità 23 – Future Forms (Will / Going to / Present Continuous)

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • usare diversi modi per parlare del futuro
    • scegliere la forma corretta in base al contesto
    • parlare in modo più naturale e fluido
    • evitare errori tipici

    🧠 Le 3 forme del futuro

    1. Going to → piani

    👉 decisione già presa

    • I am going to travel
    • I am going to study tonight

    2. Will → decisione spontanea

    👉 decido ora

    • I will help you
    • I will call you

    3. Present Continuous → futuro organizzato

    👉 appuntamenti / programmi

    • I am meeting a friend
    • I am travelling tomorrow

    ⚖️ Differenza chiave

    Forma Uso
    going to piano
    will decisione immediata
    present continuous programma organizzato

    📚 Esempi reali

    Going to

    • I am going to study tonight
      👉 già deciso

    Will

    • Ok, I will help you
      👉 decisione ora

    Present Continuous

    • I am meeting my boss tomorrow
      👉 appuntamento fissato

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Dialogue:

    A: What are you doing tomorrow?
    B: I am meeting a friend.

    A: I’m tired.
    B: I will help you.

    A: What are you going to do this weekend?
    B: I am going to travel.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • I will call you
    • I am going to study
    • I am meeting a friend

    👉 Ora prova:

    • I will ___
    • I am going to ___
    • I am ___ tomorrow

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 6 frasi:

    👉 2 will
    👉 2 going to
    👉 2 present continuous

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Forma naturale:

    • I will → I’ll
    • I am going to → I’m gonna
    • I am meeting → I’m meeting

    👉 parlato fluido = fondamentale B1

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I will to go
    ✅ I will go

    ❌ I going to travel
    ✅ I am going to travel

    ❌ I am go tomorrow
    ✅ I am going tomorrow

    ❌ I will meeting
    ✅ I will meet

    🧠 Pattern mentale

    👉 chiediti:

    • È pianificato? → going to
    • È deciso ora? → will
    • È organizzato? → present continuous

    🔁 Confronto reale

    • I am going to travel → piano
    • I am travelling tomorrow → già organizzato
    • Ok, I will travel → decisione ora

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Scegli la forma corretta:

    1. I (will / am going to) study tonight (già deciso)
    2. Ok, I (will / am going to) help you
    3. I (am meeting / will meet) my friend tomorrow
    4. I (will go / am going) now

    🔄 Trasforma

    Completa:

    1. I decided now → I ______ help
    2. I have a plan → I am ______ to travel
    3. I have a meeting → I am ______ a client

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Parla:

    👉 cosa farai domani
    👉 cosa hai già organizzato
    👉 cosa decidi ora

    🚀 Mini task

    Parla per 2 minuti:

    👉 i tuoi piani futuri

    usa: - will
    - going to
    - present continuous

    💬 Extra practice

    Rispondi:

    • What are you going to do tomorrow?
    • What will you do tonight?
    • What are you doing this weekend?

    🎯 Challenge

    Crea:

    • 3 piani
    • 2 decisioni spontanee
    • 2 appuntamenti

    Real English uses different futures, not just “will”.
