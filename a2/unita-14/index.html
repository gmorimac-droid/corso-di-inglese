    Unità 14 – Travel and Holidays

    Modalità Base attiva. Questa unità ti aiuta a parlare di viaggi e vacanze usando il Past Simple in modo semplice e naturale. Imparerai a descrivere dove sei andato, dove hai alloggiato, cosa hai visitato e cosa hai fatto durante una vacanza. Per la versione estesa usa il toggle BASE / PRO nella barra in alto.

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • parlare di un viaggio o di una vacanza passata
    • usare il Past Simple per raccontare eventi conclusi
    • usare parole utili per hotel, trasporti, città e attività turistiche
    • fare domande semplici sulle vacanze di un'altra persona

    🧠 Grammar in context

    Past Simple per raccontare un viaggio

    Usiamo il Past Simple per parlare di azioni finite nel passato.

    • I went to Spain last summer.
    • We stayed in a small hotel.
    • She visited two museums.
    • They travelled by train.

    Sequenza di eventi

    Per raccontare una vacanza in ordine logico usiamo connettivi semplici:

    • first
    • then
    • after that
    • later
    • finally

    Esempio:

    First, we arrived at the hotel. Then we went to the beach. After that, we had lunch. Later, we visited the old town. Finally, we went back to the hotel.

    Domande utili

    Per fare domande al passato usiamo did.

    • Where did you go?
    • How did you travel?
    • Where did you stay?
    • What did you do?
    • Did you enjoy it?

    📚 Core vocabulary

    Travel words

    • travel
    • trip
    • holiday
    • journey
    • ticket
    • suitcase
    • airport
    • station
    • hotel
    • hostel

    Transport

    • plane
    • train
    • bus
    • car
    • taxi
    • underground
    • ferry

    Places and activities

    • beach
    • mountains
    • city centre
    • museum
    • restaurant
    • monument
    • take photos
    • visit

    • walk around
    • buy souvenirs

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Text:

    Last August I went to Lisbon with two friends. We travelled by plane and stayed in a hotel near the city centre. During the day we visited museums, walked around the old town and took lots of photos. In the evening we ate in small restaurants and listened to live music. We really enjoyed the trip.

    Comprensione

    1. Where did the person go?
    2. Who did the person go with?
    3. How did they travel?
    4. Where did they stay?
    5. What did they do in the evening?

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • I went to Lisbon last summer.
    • We travelled by plane.
    • We stayed in a hotel.
    • We visited the old town.
    • We took a lot of photos.

    Ora completa oralmente:

    • Last summer I went to ___
    • I travelled by ___
    • I stayed in ___
    • I visited ___
    • In the evening I ___

    Mini task:

    Parla per 40–60 secondi su una vacanza che hai fatto o che vorresti fare.

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 8–10 frasi su una vacanza passata.

    Puoi includere:

    • dove sei andato
    • con chi sei andato
    • come hai viaggiato
    • dove hai alloggiato
    • cosa hai fatto
    • se ti è piaciuta

    Modello utile

    Last summer I went to Rome with my family. We travelled by train. We stayed in a small hotel. We visited many monuments. We ate in nice restaurants. We enjoyed the holiday very much.

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Attenzione alla pronuncia di alcuni verbi al passato:

    • stayed → /steɪd/
    • visited → /ˈvɪzɪtɪd/
    • walked → /wɔːkt/
    • travelled → /ˈtrævəld/

    👉 La finale -ed non si pronuncia sempre allo stesso modo.

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I go to Paris last year.
    ✅ I went to Paris last year.

    ❌ We didn't travelled by plane.
    ✅ We didn't travel by plane.

    ❌ I stayed in hotel near the beach.
    ✅ I stayed in a hotel near the beach.

    ❌ Did you went with your family?
    ✅ Did you go with your family?

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa con la forma corretta.

    1. We ___ (go) to Greece last summer.
    2. She ___ (stay) in a hostel.
    3. They ___ (travel) by bus.
    4. I ___ (visit) a museum.
    5. ___ you ___ (enjoy) the holiday?
    6. We ___ not ___ (buy) souvenirs.

    🔄 Trasforma

    Trasforma le frasi al passato.

    1. I travel by train. → ________
    2. We stay in a hotel. → ________
    3. They visit a museum. → ________
    4. Do you enjoy the trip? → ________

    Modalità Professional attiva. In questa unità impari a raccontare un viaggio in modo più credibile e più ricco: non solo dove sei andato, ma anche come ti sei mosso, che esperienza hai avuto, cosa è andato bene o male e come organizzare un piccolo travel story con sequenza, dettagli e lessico davvero utile in conversazione.

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • raccontare una vacanza o un viaggio breve con una sequenza chiara di eventi
    • usare il Past Simple con verbi ad alta frequenza legati al travel English
    • distinguere parole vicine come trip, travel, journey, holiday
    • fare e rispondere a domande più naturali su trasporto, alloggio, attività ed esperienza personale
    • produrre un breve resoconto orale o scritto con dettagli concreti e opinioni semplici ma efficaci

    🧭 Language focus – talking about travel naturally

    1. Raccontare fatti conclusi

    Il Past Simple resta il tempo centrale per descrivere una vacanza finita.

    • I went to Porto for a long weekend.
    • We stayed in a small guesthouse near the river.
    • I visited two museums and tried local food.
    • We came back on Sunday evening.

    2. Aggiungere ordine e coerenza

    Per raccontare bene non basta una lista di frasi: serve una progressione.

    • first
    • then
    • after that
    • later
    • in the evening
    • on the last day
    • finally

    Confronta:

    Lista debole:

    I went to Prague. I stayed in a hotel. I visited the centre. I ate a lot.

    Versione migliore:

    First, I flew to Prague and checked into a small hotel near the centre. Then I walked around the Old Town and visited a museum. In the evening I tried local food in a traditional restaurant. On the last day I bought a few souvenirs and flew back home.

    👉 La seconda versione non è più “difficile” grammaticalmente, ma è molto più professionale e naturale.

    🧠 Travel vocabulary with real distinctions

    trip / travel / journey / holiday

    • trip = viaggio singolo, spesso breve o definito
    • It was a business trip.

    • We went on a school trip.

    • travel = viaggiare in generale, come attività o concetto

    • I love travel.

    • We travelled around Portugal.

    • journey = spostamento da un luogo a un altro, attenzione al tragitto

    • The train journey was long.

    • We had a comfortable journey.

    • holiday = vacanza

    • We went on holiday in June.
    • It was a relaxing holiday.

    👉 In molti contesti italiani “viaggio” copre tutto, ma in inglese la scelta della parola cambia il significato.

    Accommodation

    Activities

    Useful opinion language

    • It was great.
    • It was relaxing.
    • It was crowded.
    • It was expensive.
    • The weather was amazing.
    • The hotel was comfortable.
    • The journey was tiring.

    🎧 Reading – A short city break

    Leggi il testo.

    In October I went to Budapest with a friend for four days. We travelled by plane and stayed in a small hotel not far from the centre. On the first day we walked around the city and took a boat trip on the Danube. The weather was cold but sunny, so we spent a lot of time outdoors. On the second day we visited a museum and tried some local dishes in a traditional restaurant. Unfortunately, on the last day our flight was delayed, so we waited at the airport for three hours. Even so, we really enjoyed the trip and decided to go back one day.

    Comprensione

    1. Where did the speakers go?
    2. How long did they stay there?
    3. Where did they stay?
    4. What did they do on the first day?
    5. What problem did they have on the last day?
    6. Which sentences express opinion rather than only fact?

    Language focus

    Il testo funziona bene perché contiene:

    • fatti: went, travelled, stayed, visited
    • sequenza: on the first day, on the second day, on the last day
    • valutazione: the weather was cold but sunny, we really enjoyed the trip
    • piccolo imprevisto: our flight was delayed

    Questi elementi trasformano una lista di azioni in un vero racconto.

    🗣️ Guided Speaking

    A. Short answers → full answers

    Rispondi prima in modo breve, poi amplia.

    1. Where did you go on your last holiday?
    2. Who did you go with?
    3. How did you travel?
    4. Where did you stay?
    5. What did you do during the day?
    6. Did anything go wrong?
    7. Would you like to go back?

    Esempio:

    • I went to Naples.
    • I went to Naples with two friends for a long weekend, and we stayed near the seafront.

    B. Build a travel story

    Usa questa scaletta:

    • destination
    • transport
    • accommodation
    • activities
    • food
    • one positive point
    • one problem or surprise
    • final opinion

    C. 90-second task

    Parla per 60–90 secondi su uno di questi temi:

    • My last holiday
    • A trip I really enjoyed
    • A trip that had a problem

    ✍️ Writing

    Task 1 – Guided paragraph

    Scrivi un testo di 120–150 parole su una vacanza passata.

    Devi includere:

    • almeno 5 verbi al Past Simple
    • almeno 4 parole del travel vocabulary
    • almeno 3 connettivi temporali
    • almeno 1 opinione personale
    • almeno 1 piccolo problema o imprevisto

    Task 2 – Upgrade your writing

    Controlla se il tuo testo risponde a queste domande:

    • Il lettore capisce dove sei andato e quando?
    • Hai spiegato come hai viaggiato?
    • Hai incluso due o tre attività specifiche?
    • Hai dato una valutazione personale, non solo fatti?
    • Hai usato almeno un dettaglio concreto su hotel, cibo, luogo o trasporto?

    🔊 Pronunciation and connected speech

    Final -ed

    • stayed → /d/
    • walked → /t/
    • visited → /ɪd/

    Word stress utile

    • HOtel
    • AIRport
    • MUseum
    • souveNIR

    Rhythm tip

    Le frasi di viaggio suonano più naturali se raggruppi le informazioni:

    • We stayed in a small hotel near the centre.
    • We travelled by train and arrived in the afternoon.
    • On the last day, we bought a few souvenirs.

    ⚠️ Error clinic – errori tipici degli italiani

    ❌ I made a travel to London.
    ✅ I took a trip to London. / I travelled to London.

    ❌ We did a holiday in Greece.
    ✅ We went on holiday to Greece.

    ❌ I stayed in an hotel.
    ✅ I stayed in a hotel.

    ❌ We didn't visited the museum.
    ✅ We didn't visit the museum.

    ❌ Did you enjoyed the trip?
    ✅ Did you enjoy the trip?

    ❌ I and my friend went to Paris.
    ✅ My friend and I went to Paris.

    👉 A questo livello la correttezza grammaticale conta, ma conta anche scegliere le espressioni davvero idiomatiche.

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa con la forma corretta.

    1. We ___ (go) to Vienna in March.
    2. She ___ (book) a room online.
    3. They ___ (travel) by train and ___ (arrive) late.
    4. I ___ not ___ (buy) any souvenirs.
    5. ___ you ___ (stay) near the city centre?
    6. Our flight ___ (be) delayed.
    7. We ___ (have) dinner in a traditional restaurant.
    8. He ___ (take) lots of photos.

    🔄 Transformation drill

    Riscrivi le frasi in modo più naturale.

    1. I went to Madrid. I stayed there. I visited things.
      → _______

    2. We travelled by plane. It was good.
      → _______

    3. I did a trip with my family.
      → _______

    4. Did you enjoyed your holiday?
      → _______

    5. We didn't went to the beach because it rained.
      → _______

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Rispondi in modo personale.

    • Do you prefer city breaks or beach holidays? Why?
    • Do you like travelling alone or with other people?
    • What makes a trip memorable for you?
    • Is good food important when you travel?

    🚀 Final task

    Speaking final

    Racconta una vacanza in 90 secondi seguendo questo schema:

    • where you went
    • who you went with
    • how you travelled
    • where you stayed
    • what you did
    • one good thing
    • one problem
    • your final opinion

    Writing final

    Scrivi un breve travel post intitolato:

    A holiday I still remember

    Obiettivo: testo chiaro, concreto e naturale, non solo corretto.
