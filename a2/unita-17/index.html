    Unità 17 – Quantifiers (some, any, much, many)

    🎯 Obiettivo

    Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

    • parlare di quantità in modo corretto
    • usare some e any
    • usare much e many
    • evitare errori molto comuni

    🧠 Grammar in context

    Some

    👉 usato in frasi positive

    • I have some water
    • I want some apples

    Any

    👉 usato in domande e negative

    • Do you have any water?
    • I don’t have any money

    Esempi

    • I have some coffee
    • I don’t have any coffee
    • Do you have any coffee?

    ⚖️ Much vs Many

    Many → countable (numerabili)

    • many apples
    • many books

    Much → uncountable (non numerabili)

    • much water
    • much money

    Esempi

    • I have many books
    • I don’t have much money

    📚 Core vocabulary

    Countable

    • apples
    • books
    • chairs
    • shops

    Uncountable

    • water
    • coffee
    • money
    • food

    🎧 Listening / Reading

    Dialogue:

    A: Do you have any money?
    B: No, I don’t have any money.

    A: Do you have any apples?
    B: Yes, I have some apples.

    🗣️ Speaking

    Ripeti:

    • I have some water
    • I don’t have any money
    • Do you have any food?

    👉 Ora prova:

    • I have some ___
    • I don’t have any ___
    • Do you have any ___?

    ✍️ Writing

    Scrivi 5 frasi:

    1. I have some ______
    2. I don’t have any ______
    3. Do you have any ______?
    4. I have many ______
    5. I don’t have much ______

    🔊 Pronunciation

    Attenzione:

    • some → /sʌm/
    • any → /ˈeni/
    • many → /ˈmeni/

    👉 suoni diversi → non confondere

    ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

    ❌ I don’t have some money
    ✅ I don’t have any money

    ❌ I have many money
    ✅ I have much money

    ❌ I have much apples
    ✅ I have many apples

    🧠 Pattern fondamentale

    • positive → some
    • negative → any
    • question → any

    🔁 Schema semplice

    Tipo Uso
    positive some
    negative any
    question any
    countable many
    uncountable much

    ✅ Review & Practice

    Completa:

    1. I have ___ water
    2. I don’t have ___ money
    3. Do you have ___ food?
    4. I have ___ apples
    5. I don’t have ___ time

    🔄 Trasforma

    Completa:

    1. I have water → I have ___ water
    2. I don’t have money → I don’t have ___ money
    3. apples → I have ___ apples

    🧠 Think & Speak

    Parla:

    👉 cosa hai
    👉 cosa non hai

    🚀 Mini task

    Simula:

    👉 sei al supermercato

    • I have some ___
    • I need some ___
    • I don’t have any ___

    💬 Extra practice

    Rispondi:

    • Do you have any money?
    • Do you have any food?
    • How many apples do you have?

    🎯 Challenge

    Aggiungi:

    • 3 frasi con some
    • 3 frasi con any
    • 2 con many
    • 2 con much

    Natural English depends on small words like some and any.
