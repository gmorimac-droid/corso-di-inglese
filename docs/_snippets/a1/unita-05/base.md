## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Forma base

Usiamo il **Present Simple** per parlare di:

- abitudini
- routine
- fatti generali

Schema:

**subject + verb**

- I work in an office.
- You live in Rome.
- They study English.

---

### Terza persona: **he / she / it**

Con **he / she / it** normalmente aggiungiamo **-s** al verbo.

- He works in a bank.
- She lives in Milan.
- It starts at 9.

---

### Esempi utili

- I drink coffee in the morning.
- We study English every day.
- He works from home.
- She eats lunch at 1.
- My brother plays football on Saturday.

## 🔊 Pronunciation

### La **-s** finale

Ascolta e ripeti:

- works
- lives
- eats
- plays

👉 Non dimenticare la **-s** con **he / she / it**.

❌ He work in a bank.  
✅ He works in a bank.

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni

❌ He work every day.  
✅ He works every day.

❌ She live in Rome.  
✅ She lives in Rome.

❌ I am work in Milan.  
✅ I work in Milan.

👉 Con i verbi normali **non usare “am / is / are”**.
