## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare il verbo **to be** al presente
- fare frasi affermative, negative e interrogative
- dare informazioni semplici su identità, provenienza e professione
- rispondere con **short answers**

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Forma affermativa

| Subject | Verb |
|---|---|
| I | am |
| You | are |
| He | is |
| She | is |
| It | is |
| We | are |
| They | are |

### Esempi

- I am a student.
- You are my friend.
- She is from Italy.
- They are teachers.

### Forma negativa

Struttura:

**Subject + to be + not**

- I am not tired.
- He is not a doctor.
- We are not at home.

### Forma interrogativa

Struttura:

**To be + subject ... ?**

- Are you a student?
- Is she Italian?
- Are they ready?

### Risposte brevi

- Yes, I am. / No, I’m not.
- Yes, he is. / No, he isn’t.
- Yes, they are. / No, they aren’t.

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: Are you a student?
B: Yes, I am.
A: Are you from Italy?
B: Yes, I am. What about you?
A: I’m from Spain.
B: Are you a teacher?
A: No, I’m not. I’m an engineer.

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti ad alta voce:

- I’m a student.
- I’m from Italy.
- She isn’t a teacher.
- Are they ready?
- Yes, they are.

👉 Poi rispondi:

- Are you tired?
- Are you from Italy?
- Are you a student?

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Contrazioni molto usate:

- I am → **I’m**
- You are → **You’re**
- He is → **He’s**
- She is → **She’s**
- We are → **We’re**
- They are → **They’re**

👉 Nel parlato naturale si usano spesso le contrazioni.

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni

❌ I have 25 years old  
✅ I am 25 years old

❌ She are Italian  
✅ She is Italian

❌ Are from Rome?  
✅ Are you from Rome?
