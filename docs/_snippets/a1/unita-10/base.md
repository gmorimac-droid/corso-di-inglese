## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- ripassare le strutture principali del livello A1
- parlare di te in modo semplice ma continuo
- fare e capire domande personali molto comuni
- descrivere una routine, un luogo e alcune abilità

---

## 🧠 Grammar Review

In A1 hai studiato:

- **to be** → I am, you are, she is
- **Present Simple** → I work, he works
- **WH questions** → What, Where, How old, When
- **this / that / these / those**
- **there is / there are**
- **can / can't**

Ora il punto non è ricordare una regola alla volta, ma **usarle insieme** in una situazione reale.

## 📝 TEST 2 – Trasformazioni

Trasforma le frasi come richiesto.

1. I am a teacher. → negativa  
2. She works in a shop. → domanda  
3. There is a park near my house. → plurale  
4. You can drive. → negativa  
5. This is my book. → plurale  
6. They are from Spain. → domanda

## 🎧 Reading

Leggi il testo.

> Hello, my name is Sofia and I am 24 years old. I am from Italy and I live in a small town. I work in a café. Every day I wake up at 6:30, have breakfast and go to work by bus. In my room there is a bed, a desk and a small lamp. In my town there is a bank, a supermarket and a park. I can cook quite well, but I can't drive.

### Comprensione

Rispondi:

1. How old is Sofia?
2. Where is she from?
3. Where does she work?
4. What is in her room?
5. Can she drive?

## 🗣️ Speaking Test

Parla per **1 minuto** senza fermarti troppo.

Devi includere:

- name
- age
- country / city
- job or studies
- daily routine
- one description of a room or town
- two things you can do
- one thing you can't do

### Modello utile

> Hi, my name is ...  
> I am ... years old and I am from ...  
> I work / study ...  
> Every day I wake up at ...  
> In my room there is ... / In my town there are ...  
> I can ..., but I can't ...

## ⚠️ Errori comuni finali

❌ I have 25 years.  
✅ I am 25 years old.

❌ He work in an office.  
✅ He works in an office.

❌ There is two chairs.  
✅ There are two chairs.

❌ I can to swim.  
✅ I can swim.

❌ Where you live?  
✅ Where do you live?
