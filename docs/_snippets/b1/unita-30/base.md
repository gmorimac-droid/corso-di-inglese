- modi per **esprimere opinioni**
- lessico e strutture per **work and study**

## 📝 TEST 1 – Mixed Grammar

Completa le frasi con la forma corretta.

1. I have never ___ in a multinational company. *(work)*
2. She ___ to Berlin last year. *(go)*
3. Look at those clouds! It ___ rain. *(going to)*
4. If you study regularly, you ___ feel more confident. *(will)*
5. You ___ wear a helmet here. It’s the rule. *(must / have to)*
6. You ___ bring food to the meeting if you don’t want to. *(don’t have to / mustn’t)*
7. I think online courses are ___ useful than people think. *(more)*
8. Can we ___ the meeting off until Friday? *(phrasal verb)*
9. I’m sorry for the delay. I’ll ___ back to you tomorrow. *(phrasal verb)*
10. I haven’t finished the report ___. *(yet / already)*

---

## 📝 TEST 2 – Transformations

Trasforma le frasi come indicato.

1. I visited London in 2022. → *(experience)*
2. We’ve finished the task. → *(specific past time: yesterday)*
3. I plan to start a new course in September. → *(going to)*
4. Maybe it will be difficult. → *(opinion sentence)*
5. If you don’t hurry, you’ll miss the train. → *(negative result condition)*
6. You mustn’t park here. → *(explain the rule in your own words)*
7. I’ll contact you later. → *(phrasal verb)*
8. I don’t agree. → *(softer opinion)*

## 🗣️ SPEAKING REVIEW

Parla per **3 minuti** e includi:

- chi sei e cosa fai
- una tua esperienza importante
- un’opinione su studio, lavoro o tecnologia
- una regola o consiglio utile
- un piano futuro
- una situazione con **if**

### Traccia possibile

1. personal introduction
2. work / study situation
3. life experience
4. opinion and reason
5. advice or obligation
6. future plan
7. one conditional sentence

---

## ✍️ WRITING TASK

Scrivi un testo di **140–170 parole** su uno di questi temi:

- **My work or study life and my future plans**
- **Why learning English is useful today**
- **A professional situation I would like to manage well in English**

Il testo deve includere:

- almeno **1 Present Perfect**
- almeno **1 Past Simple**
- almeno **1 future form**
- almeno **1 opinion**
- almeno **1 modal**
- almeno **1 phrasal verb**

---

## 🧠 THINK & SPEAK

Rispondi oralmente:

- Have you ever had to use English in a real situation?
- What did you do last weekend?
- What are you going to do next month?
- What should a good student do to improve quickly?
- What do you think makes communication effective?
- If you had more time, what would you study more?

---

## 🔊 PRONUNCIATION CHECK

Leggi ad alta voce:

- I’ve already sent the email.
- I went to a conference last month.
- We’re meeting the client tomorrow.
- If you need help, I’ll call you back.
- You don’t have to stay, but you mustn’t be late next time.

Fai attenzione a:

- contrazioni come **I’ve**, **I’ll**, **we’re**
- differenza tra **worked** /t/ e **needed** /ɪd/
- collegamento naturale tra le parole

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni

❌ I have seen him yesterday.
✅ I saw him yesterday.

❌ If it will rain, we will stay home.
✅ If it rains, we will stay home.

❌ You mustn’t come if you don’t want.
✅ You don’t have to come if you don’t want.

❌ I very agree.
✅ I completely agree.

❌ I will contact to you tomorrow.
✅ I will contact you tomorrow.
