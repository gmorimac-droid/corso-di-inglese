---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

In questa unità non studiamo una sola regola grammaticale isolata. L’obiettivo è **combinare più strutture utili** per parlare di lavoro e studio in modo realistico.

Le strutture più utili sono:

- **Present Simple** per routine e responsabilità abituali
- **Present Continuous** per progetti temporanei o attività in corso
- **Present Perfect** per esperienza e durata
- **Future forms** per piani professionali o accademici

### Esempi

- I work for an international company.
- I’m currently working on a new project.
- I have worked in customer service for three years.
- I’m going to apply for a new position next month.

## 1. Talking about your job

### Useful patterns

- I work as a sales assistant.
- I work in marketing.
- I work for a small company.
- I work with clients from different countries.
- I’m responsible for customer support.

### Difference to remember

- **work as** + professione → I work as a teacher.
- **work in** + settore / area → I work in finance.
- **work for** + azienda / organizzazione → I work for a multinational company.
- **work with** + persone / strumenti / clienti → I work with children / data / international teams.

## 🎧 Reading / Dialogue

**Dialogue 1 – Professional introduction**

A: So, what do you do?
B: I work as a project coordinator for a logistics company.
A: What does your job involve?
B: I organise schedules, deal with suppliers and support the operations team.
A: How long have you been there?
B: I’ve worked there for almost three years.

**Dialogue 2 – Talking about studies**

A: What are you studying at the moment?
B: I’m studying business administration.
A: Do you enjoy it?
B: Yes, especially the management modules, although some subjects are quite demanding.
A: What would you like to do after graduation?
B: I’d like to work in human resources.

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi un testo di **100–120 parole** su uno di questi temi:

1. **My current job**
2. **My studies and future plans**
3. **My work experience so far**

Cerca di includere:

- il tuo ruolo o corso di studi
- almeno 3 responsabilità o attività
- qualcosa che ti piace o non ti piace
- un piano futuro

### Example

> I work for a small company in the tourism sector. I mainly answer emails, help clients and organise bookings. I enjoy the job because it is dynamic and I speak to many people every day. However, it can be stressful during busy periods. I have worked there for two years, and now I’m doing an online English course because I’d like to take on more international tasks in the future.

- **career** → /kəˈrɪr/
- **colleague** → /ˈkɒliːɡ/

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I do a work in an office  
✅ I work in an office

❌ I make experience in this field  
✅ I have experience in this field

❌ I study to university  
✅ I study at university

❌ I am responsible to manage the team  
✅ I am responsible for managing the team

❌ I have 28 years and I work from 5 years here  
✅ I’m 28 and I have worked here for five years
