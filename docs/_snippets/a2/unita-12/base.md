## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- distinguere tra **routine** e **azioni in corso**
- scegliere tra **Present Simple** e **Present Continuous**
- usare correttamente avverbi ed espressioni di tempo
- evitare gli errori più comuni quando parli di “adesso” e di “sempre”

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Present Simple

Usiamo il **Present Simple** per:

- abitudini
- routine
- fatti generali
- situazioni stabili

Esempi:

- I work in a hotel.
- She drinks coffee every morning.
- They live in Naples.
- We usually study in the evening.

---

### Present Continuous

Usiamo il **Present Continuous** per:

- azioni che stanno succedendo adesso
- situazioni temporanee
- attività in corso in questo periodo

Esempi:

- I am working now.
- She is drinking tea at the moment.
- They are living with friends this month.
- We are studying for an exam this week.

---

## ⚖️ Differenza chiave

| Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|---|---|
| routine | adesso / in questo periodo |
| every day | now |
| usually | at the moment |
| always | today / this week |

Confronta:

- I work from home. → in generale
- I am working from home today. → oggi, temporaneamente

- She studies English on Mondays. → routine
- She is studying English right now. → in questo momento

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: What do you do in the evenings?
B: I usually study English after dinner.
A: And what are you doing now?
B: I’m doing my homework.
A: Do you always study at home?
B: No, not always. Sometimes I study at the library.

### Comprensione

1. What does B usually do in the evenings?
2. What is B doing now?
3. Does B always study at home?

Ripeti:

Ora completa oralmente:

- I usually ___ in the morning.
- I am ___ right now.

- My friend is ___ today.

Fai domande:

- What do you usually do after work?
- What are you doing now?

- Are you studying English at the moment?

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi **8 frasi**:

- 4 frasi con il **Present Simple** sulla tua routine
- 4 frasi con il **Present Continuous** su adesso o su questo periodo

### Modello utile

> I usually wake up at seven.
> I drink coffee every morning.
> I work from Monday to Friday.
> I often read before bed.
>
> I am sitting at my desk now.
> I am writing in English.
> I am using my computer.
> I am studying this lesson today.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Ascolta la differenza tra:

- work / working
- live / living
- study / studying

👉 Nel **Present Continuous** la parte importante è la pronuncia chiara di **am / is / are** e della finale **-ing**.

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I am working every day.  
✅ I work every day.

❌ I work now.  
✅ I am working now.

❌ She is usually drinking coffee in the morning.  
✅ She usually drinks coffee in the morning.

❌ They living in Rome this month.  
✅ They are living in Rome this month.

## ✅ Review & Practice

Scegli la forma corretta:

1. I (work / am working) in a bank.
2. I (work / am working) now.
3. She (usually goes / is usually going) to school by bus.
4. We (study / are studying) for a test this week.
5. They (live / are living) in Milan, but this month they (stay / are staying) with friends.

## 🔄 Trasforma

Trasforma le frasi secondo l’indicazione.

1. I work in an office. → (now) ____________________
2. She is studying now. → (every day) ____________________
3. They play football on Saturdays. → (today) ____________________
4. We are having lunch now. → (usually) ____________________
