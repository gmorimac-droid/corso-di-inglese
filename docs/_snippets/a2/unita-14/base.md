Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- parlare di un viaggio o di una vacanza passata

- usare parole utili per hotel, trasporti, città e attività turistiche
- fare domande semplici sulle vacanze di un'altra persona

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Past Simple per raccontare un viaggio

Usiamo il **Past Simple** per parlare di azioni finite nel passato.

- I went to Spain last summer.
- We stayed in a small hotel.
- She visited two museums.
- They travelled by train.

---

### Sequenza di eventi

Per raccontare una vacanza in ordine logico usiamo connettivi semplici:

- first
- then
- after that
- later
- finally

Esempio:

> First, we arrived at the hotel. Then we went to the beach. After that, we had lunch. Later, we visited the old town. Finally, we went back to the hotel.

---

### Domande utili

Per fare domande al passato usiamo **did**.

- Where did you go?
- How did you travel?
- Where did you stay?
- What did you do?
- Did you enjoy it?

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Text:**

> Last August I went to Lisbon with two friends. We travelled by plane and stayed in a hotel near the city centre. During the day we visited museums, walked around the old town and took lots of photos. In the evening we ate in small restaurants and listened to live music. We really enjoyed the trip.

### Comprensione

1. Where did the person go?
2. Who did the person go with?
3. How did they travel?
4. Where did they stay?
5. What did they do in the evening?

Mini task:

Parla per **40–60 secondi** su una vacanza che hai fatto o che vorresti fare.

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi **8–10 frasi** su una vacanza passata.

Puoi includere:

- dove sei andato
- con chi sei andato
- come hai viaggiato
- dove hai alloggiato
- cosa hai fatto
- se ti è piaciuta

### Modello utile

> Last summer I went to Rome with my family.
> We travelled by train.
> We stayed in a small hotel.
> We visited many monuments.
> We ate in nice restaurants.
> We enjoyed the holiday very much.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione alla pronuncia di alcuni verbi al passato:

- stayed → /steɪd/
- visited → /ˈvɪzɪtɪd/
- walked → /wɔːkt/
- travelled → /ˈtrævəld/

👉 La finale **-ed** non si pronuncia sempre allo stesso modo.

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I go to Paris last year.  
✅ I went to Paris last year.

❌ We didn't travelled by plane.  
✅ We didn't travel by plane.

❌ I stayed in hotel near the beach.  
✅ I stayed in a hotel near the beach.

❌ Did you went with your family?  
✅ Did you go with your family?
