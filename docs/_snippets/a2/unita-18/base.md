## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- parlare di piani futuri con **be going to**
- esprimere decisioni prese sul momento con **will**
- fare domande e frasi negative sul futuro
- usare espressioni di tempo come **tomorrow**, **next week**, **tonight**, **later**
- evitare errori comuni come **I will to go** o **I going to study**

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Be going to → piani e intenzioni

Usiamo **be going to** quando parliamo di qualcosa che abbiamo già deciso o che intendiamo fare.

- I am going to study tonight.
- She is going to visit her grandparents.
- We are going to travel next month.

Schema:

- **I am going to + verbo**
- **He / She is going to + verbo**
- **We / They are going to + verbo**

Esempi:

- I am going to cook dinner.
- They are going to watch a film.
- He is going to start a new course.

---

### Will → decisioni spontanee

Usiamo **will** quando decidiamo qualcosa nel momento in cui parliamo.

- I will help you.
- OK, I will call her now.
- Don't worry, we will wait.

Schema:

- **I / You / He / She / We / They will + verbo**

Forma contratta:

- I'll help.
- You'll see.
- We'll go later.

---

### Negative and questions

#### Going to

- I am not going to work tomorrow.
- She isn't going to come.
- Are you going to study tonight?
- Is he going to buy a new phone?

#### Will

- I will not call him now.
- She won't be late.
- Will you help me?
- Will they come with us?

---

### Differenza base

| Struttura | Uso principale |
|----------|-----------------|
| **be going to** | piano / intenzione già decisa |
| **will** | decisione presa sul momento |

Esempi:

- I am going to visit Rome next weekend. → l'ho già deciso
- OK, I will come with you. → lo decido adesso

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue 1 – making plans**

A: What are you going to do this weekend?
B: I am going to visit my sister.
A: Nice. Are you going to stay there all weekend?
B: No, just for one day.

**Dialogue 2 – spontaneous decision**

A: I can't open this box.
B: Wait, I will help you.
A: Thanks.
B: No problem.

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi **8 frasi**:

- 4 con **be going to**
- 4 con **will**

Puoi seguire questo modello:

> I am going to study English tonight.  
> We are going to visit our friends on Saturday.  
> She is going to buy a new bag next week.  
> They are going to stay at home tomorrow.  
>  
> I will help you with your homework.  
> I will call you later.  
> We will wait here.  
> He will open the window.

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione a queste forme naturali:

- **going to** → spesso si sente **gonna** nel parlato informale
- **I will** → **I'll**
- **we will** → **we'll**
- **will not** → **won't**

Esempi:

- I'm going to go. → nel parlato informale può suonare come **I'm gonna go**
- I'll call you later.
- We won't be late.

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I will to go  
✅ I will go

❌ I going to study  
✅ I am going to study

❌ I will going  
✅ I will go

❌ She going to work tomorrow  
✅ She is going to work tomorrow

❌ Are you going study tonight?  
✅ Are you going to study tonight?

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentali

- **be going to + verbo base**
- **will + verbo base**
- **be** cambia in base al soggetto
- dopo **will** non usiamo mai **to**

Esempi:

- I am going to study.
- He is going to work.
- They are going to travel.
- I will help.
- We will call later.
