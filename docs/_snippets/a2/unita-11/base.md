## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Forma affermativa

Usiamo il **Present Continuous** per parlare di qualcosa che sta succedendo adesso.

**Subject + to be + verb-ing**

- I am working.
- You are studying.
- She is reading.
- They are watching TV.

👉 Il verbo principale prende la forma **-ing**.

---

### Forma negativa

**Subject + to be + not + verb-ing**

- I am not working.
- He is not sleeping.
- We are not studying.

Forme contratte più comuni:

- I'm not
- isn't
- aren't

Esempi:

- She isn't listening.
- They aren't eating.

---

### Forma interrogativa

**To be + subject + verb-ing?**

- Are you working?
- Is she studying?
- Are they coming?

Risposte brevi:

- Yes, I am.
- No, I'm not.
- Yes, she is.
- No, they aren't.

---

## 🔁 Present Simple vs Present Continuous

Confronta:

- I work every day. → routine
- I am working now. → adesso

Altri esempi:

- She studies in the evening. → abitudine
- She is studying now. → in questo momento

👉 **now / right now / at the moment** aiutano spesso a capire che serve il Present Continuous.

## ✍️ Spelling con -ing

Osserva questi cambiamenti:

- work → working
- read → reading
- make → making
- write → writing
- sit → sitting
- run → running

Regole base:

- di solito aggiungi **-ing**: work → working
- se il verbo finisce in **-e**, togli la **-e**: make → making
- in alcuni verbi brevi raddoppi la consonante finale: sit → sitting

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: Hi Anna, what are you doing?
B: I'm doing my English homework.
A: Are you studying alone?
B: No, I'm studying with my brother.
A: Is he doing the same exercise?
B: No, he isn't. He's writing an email.

### Comprensione

1. What is Anna doing?
2. Is she studying alone?
3. What is her brother doing?

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I am studying now.
- She is writing an email.
- We are waiting for the bus.
- They aren't sleeping.
- Are you listening?

Ora completa oralmente:

- I am ___ right now.
- My friend is ___ at the moment.
- We are ___ today.

Fai domande:

- What are you doing?
- Are you working now?
- Is your teacher speaking English?

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi **6–8 frasi** su cosa sta succedendo intorno a te adesso.

Puoi includere:

- cosa stai facendo tu
- cosa fanno altre persone
- cosa non sta succedendo
- dove ti trovi

### Modello utile

> I am sitting at my desk.  
> I am studying English.  
> My phone is not ringing.  
> People are walking outside.  
> My family is having dinner.

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione alla finale **-ing**:

- working → /ˈwɜːrkɪŋ/
- reading → /ˈriːdɪŋ/
- sitting → /ˈsɪtɪŋ/

👉 Il suono finale è **/ɪŋ/**, non “ingh” italiano.

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I am work now.  
✅ I am working now.

❌ She working.  
✅ She is working.

❌ Are studying?  
✅ Are you studying?

❌ I work now. *(se parli di questo momento)*  
✅ I am working now.
