# Unità 39 – Advanced Speaking (Storytelling & Arguments)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- raccontare storie in modo chiaro e interessante
- strutturare un discorso
- argomentare le tue idee
- parlare più a lungo senza perdere il filo

---

## 🧠 Perché è importante?

👉 livello B1:

- Yesterday I went out. It was nice.  

---

👉 livello B2:

- Yesterday I went out with friends and we had a great time because the place was really nice.  

👉 più ricco ✔️  

---

## 📖 Storytelling (raccontare storie)

### Struttura base

1. Introduzione  
2. Evento  
3. Dettagli  
4. Conclusione  

---

## 📚 Esempio

> Yesterday I went out with my friends.  
> We went to a restaurant in the city.  
> The food was amazing and the atmosphere was great.  
> In the end, we had a really good time.

---

## 🔗 Linking words (fondamentali)

- first  
- then  
- after that  
- finally  

---

### Esempio

> First, we met at 8. Then we went to a restaurant. After that, we walked in the city. Finally, we went home.

---

## 🧠 Adding details

👉 rende il discorso interessante

- very  
- really  
- a lot  
- quite  

---

### Esempi

- It was very interesting  
- The food was really good  

---

## 🧩 Giving arguments

👉 esprimere e difendere idee

---

### Struttura

- I think...  
- because...  
- for example...  

---

### Esempio

> I think travelling is important because you can learn new cultures. For example, when I went to Spain, I learned a lot.

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Text:**

> First, I went to the city. Then I met my friends. After that, we had dinner. Finally, we went home.  
> I think it was a great experience because I relaxed and enjoyed my time.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- First...  
- Then...  
- After that...  
- Finally...  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- First I ___  
- Then I ___  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi:

👉 una storia (6–8 frasi)

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

👉 importante:

- ritmo continuo  
- collegare le frasi  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ frasi troppo brevi  
❌ niente connessioni  
❌ ripetizione  

👉 soluzione:
- usare linking words  
- aggiungere dettagli  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

👉 evento → dettaglio → risultato  

---

## 🔁 Upgrade (B2 reale)

👉 combina:

- storytelling + opinione  

---

### Esempio

> I went to a new restaurant and it was great. I think it’s one of the best places in the city because the food was excellent.

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Completa:

1. First, I ___  
2. Then I ___  
3. I think ___ because ___  

---

## 🔄 Trasforma

Espandi:

1. I went out → ______  
2. It was good → ______  
3. I liked it → ______  

---

## 🧠 Think & Speak

Parla:

👉 racconta:

- un’esperienza  
- una giornata  
- un viaggio  

---

## 🚀 Mini task

Parla per 3–4 minuti:

👉 racconta una storia completa  

usa:
- first / then / after that / finally  
- because  

---

## 💬 Extra practice

Rispondi:

- What did you do last weekend?  
- Tell me about a good experience  
- Why was it good?

👉 4 minuti senza fermarti
