## 📝 TEST 1 – Grammar & Vocabulary

Completa:

1. I have ___ (finish) my work  
2. Yesterday I ___ (go) out  
3. If I ___ (have) time, I will go  
4. I think it’s good ___ it is useful  
5. I made a ___ (decision)  
6. I have a friend ___ lives in London  
7. I am going ___ (travel) tomorrow  
8. You should ___ (study) more  
9. I ___ up at 7 (phrasal verb)  
10. I would like ___ (ask) you something  

---

## 📝 TEST 2 – Transformation

Completa:

1. I want help → I would like ______  
2. I have a friend. He works → I have a friend ______  
3. I went out. It was nice → ______  
4. It is expensive → It ______ (paraphrase)  

---

## 🗣️ SPEAKING TEST (FONDAMENTALE)

👉 Parla per 5 minuti

Devi includere:

- introduzione personale  
- routine  
- esperienza passata  
- opinione  
- discussione (pro / contro)  
- futuro  

---

### Esempio

> Hi, my name is Marco and I live in Italy.  
> I work in an office and I study English in my free time.  
> Recently, I have improved my English a lot.  
> I think learning English is very important because it gives you more opportunities.  
> However, it can be difficult at the beginning.  
> In the future, I am going to travel more and use English every day.

---

## ✍️ WRITING TEST

Scrivi un testo (15–20 frasi):

Deve includere:

- introduzione  
- sviluppo  
- conclusione  

👉 usa:

- because  
- however  
- for this reason

👉 fluido e naturale

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (check finale)

❌ I am agree  
✅ I agree  

❌ I have done yesterday  
✅ I did it yesterday  

❌ If I will go  
✅ If I go  

❌ I make a photo  
✅ I take a photo
