# Unità 32 – Complex Sentences (and, but, because, although)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- costruire frasi più lunghe e naturali
- collegare idee in modo fluido
- esprimere contrasto e causa
- parlare come un vero livello intermedio-alto

---

## 🧠 Perché è importante?

👉 livello base:

- I like it  
- It is good  

👉 livello B2:

- I like it because it is interesting  
- It is good, but it is expensive  

---

## 🔗 I connettori principali

### AND → aggiungere

- I work and I study  
- I like coffee and tea  

---

### BUT → contrasto

- I like it, but it is expensive  
- I want to go, but I am tired  

---

### BECAUSE → causa

- I study because I want to improve  
- I am tired because I worked a lot  

---

### ALTHOUGH → contrasto forte

- Although I am tired, I will go  
- Although it is expensive, I like it  

---

## ⚖️ Differenza BUT vs ALTHOUGH

- but → più semplice  
- although → più formale / avanzato  

---

### Esempi

- I like it, but it is expensive  
- Although it is expensive, I like it  

---

## 📚 Esempi reali

- I work during the day and I study in the evening  
- I like this city, but it is very busy  
- I study English because I need it for work  
- Although it is difficult, I enjoy learning English  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Text:**

> I work a lot, but I like my job because it is interesting.  
> Although it is sometimes stressful, I learn many things.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I like it because...  
- I like it, but...  
- Although...  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I ___ because ___  
- I ___ but ___  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6 frasi:

👉 usa:

- and  
- but  
- because  
- although  

Esempio:

> I work and I study.  
> I like this job, but it is difficult.  
> I study because I want to improve.  
> Although it is hard, I continue.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- because → /bɪˈkɒz/  
- although → /ɔːlˈðəʊ/  

👉 collega le parole (linking)

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ Although but  
✅ usa solo uno  

❌ I like it because is good  
✅ I like it because it is good  

❌ I study because for work  
✅ I study because I need it for work  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

👉 struttura:

- idea + connector + idea  

---

## 🔁 Upgrade (B2 reale)

👉 combina più idee:

- I like this city because it is beautiful, but it is expensive
