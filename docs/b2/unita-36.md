# Unità 36 – Advanced Writing (Paragraphs & Structure)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- scrivere paragrafi chiari e strutturati
- organizzare le idee in modo logico
- usare connettori avanzati
- scrivere testi più naturali e professionali

---

## 🧠 Problema tipico

👉 livello basso:

- frasi scollegate  
- idee confuse  

---

👉 livello B2:

- testo organizzato  
- idee collegate  
- struttura chiara  

---

## 🧩 Struttura di un paragrafo

### 1. Topic sentence

👉 idea principale

- I think travelling is very important  

---

### 2. Supporting ideas

👉 spiegazioni

- because you can learn new cultures  
- and meet new people  

---

### 3. Conclusion

👉 chiusura

- so it is a very useful experience  

---

## 📚 Esempio completo

> I think travelling is very important because you can learn new cultures and meet new people. For this reason, it is a very useful experience.

---

## 🔗 Linking words (B2)

- first  
- then  
- also  
- however  
- for this reason  
- in conclusion  

---

## 📚 Esempi

- First, I studied English. Then I worked abroad.  
- It is useful. However, it is difficult.  
- For this reason, I continue learning.  

---

## 🎧 Reading

**Text:**

> I think learning English is important because it helps you find better opportunities. Also, you can travel more easily. For this reason, many people study English.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking → Writing bridge

👉 prima parla → poi scrivi  

- I think...  
- because...  
- so...  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi un paragrafo (5–7 frasi):

👉 tema:

- English  
- work  
- travel  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

👉 utile per scrittura:

- leggere ad alta voce  
→ controlla se suona naturale  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ frasi troppo corte  
❌ ripetizione (very very very)  
❌ nessuna connessione  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

👉 idea → spiegazione → conclusione  

---

## 🔁 Upgrade (B2 reale)

👉 usa:

- because  
- however  
- for this reason  

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Completa:

1. I think ___ because ___  
2. It is useful. However, ___  
3. For this reason, ___  

---

## 🔄 Trasforma

Unisci:

1. I like English. It is useful → ______  
2. It is difficult. I continue → ______  
3. I study. I want to improve → ______
