# Unità 37 – Relative Clauses (who, which, that)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- unire frasi in modo naturale
- descrivere persone, cose e luoghi meglio
- usare *who*, *which*, *that*
- parlare in modo più fluido e sofisticato

---

## 🧠 Perché è importante?

👉 livello base:

- I have a friend. He lives in London  

---

👉 livello B2:

- I have a friend who lives in London  

👉 più naturale ✔️  

---

## 🧩 Relative Clauses

### WHO → persone

- I have a friend who works in an office  
- She is the person who helped me  

---

### WHICH → cose

- This is a book which is very interesting  
- I have a phone which is very fast  

---

### THAT → persone + cose (più naturale)

- I have a friend that works in an office  
- This is a book that I like  

---

## 📚 Esempi reali

- The man who works here is very friendly  
- The car which I bought is expensive  
- The job that I have is interesting  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Text:**

> I have a friend who lives in Spain.  
> He has a job that is very interesting.  
> He works for a company which sells technology.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I know a person who...  
- I have something that...  
- This is a place which...  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I have a friend who ___  
- I like things that ___  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 5 frasi:

👉 usa:

- who  
- which  
- that  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

👉 importante:

- who → /huː/  
- which → /wɪtʃ/  

👉 spesso “that” è debole

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I have a friend he works  
✅ I have a friend who works  

❌ The thing what I like  
✅ The thing that I like  

❌ The person which works  
✅ The person who works  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

👉 nome + who/which/that + informazione  

---

## 🔁 Upgrade (B2 reale)

👉 usa per evitare ripetizioni:

❌ I have a car. The car is fast  
✅ I have a car that is fast  

---

## 🧠 Strategia

👉 invece di:

- 2 frasi  
👉 usa:
- 1 frase più naturale  

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Completa:

1. I have a friend ___ works in London  
2. This is a book ___ I like  
3. The person ___ helped me is here
