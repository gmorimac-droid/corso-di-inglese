# Unità 35 – Discussion Skills (Agree, Disagree, Argue)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- esprimere accordo e disaccordo
- reagire alle opinioni degli altri
- sostenere un’idea
- partecipare a discussioni reali

---

## 🧠 Perché è importante?

👉 livello B1:

- I think it’s good  

👉 livello B2:

- I agree, but I think it could be better  

👉 interazione vera

---

## 📚 Agreement (accordo)

- I agree  
- I totally agree  
- You’re right  
- That’s true  

---

### Esempi

- I agree with you  
- You’re right, it’s a good idea  

---

## 📚 Disagreement (disaccordo)

👉 soft (più naturale)

- I’m not sure  
- I don’t think so  
- I see your point, but...  

---

👉 strong

- I don’t agree  
- I think you’re wrong  

---

### Esempi

- I see your point, but I think it’s expensive  
- I don’t think that’s a good idea  

---

## 📚 Giving reasons

👉 per argomentare

- because  
- I think... because...  
- the reason is...  

---

### Esempi

- I think it’s good because it’s useful  
- I don’t agree because it’s too expensive  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: I think working from home is better.  
B: I see your point, but I prefer working in an office.  

A: Why?  
B: Because I can focus better.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I agree  
- I don’t agree  
- I see your point, but...  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I think ___  
- I agree, but ___  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6 frasi:

👉 3 accordi  
👉 3 disaccordi  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- agree → /əˈɡriː/  
- point → /pɔɪnt/  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I am agree  
✅ I agree  

❌ I don’t agree with this idea because is bad  
✅ I don’t agree because it is bad  

❌ I think yes  
✅ I think so  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- I agree, but...  
- I see your point, but...  

👉 chiave per discussioni naturali

---

## 🔁 Upgrade (B2 reale)

👉 aggiungi:

- actually  
- in my opinion  

---

### Esempi

- Actually, I don’t agree  
- In my opinion, it’s better  

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Completa:

1. I ___ with you  
2. I don’t ___  
3. I see your ___, but...  
4. I think ___ because ___  

---

## 🔄 Trasforma

Completa:

1. It is good → I agree, ______  
2. It is bad → I don’t think ______  
3. It is expensive → I see your point, ______
