# Unità 38 – Nuance & Tone (Formal vs Informal English)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- distinguere tra inglese formale e informale
- scegliere il tono corretto in base al contesto
- adattare il tuo linguaggio
- sembrare più naturale e professionale

---

## 🧠 Perché è importante?

👉 stessa idea → modi diversi

- informale: Hi, can you help me?  
- formale: Could you please help me?  

👉 cambia il tono, non il significato

---

## 📚 Informal English

👉 usato con:

- amici  
- colleghi informali  
- chat  

---

### Esempi

- Hi!  
- Thanks  
- I want  
- Let’s meet  

---

## 📚 Formal English

👉 usato per:

- lavoro  
- email professionali  
- situazioni ufficiali  

---

### Esempi

- Dear Sir/Madam  
- Thank you very much  
- I would like  
- I look forward to...  

---

## ⚖️ Confronto diretto

| Informale | Formale |
|----------|--------|
| Hi | Dear |
| Thanks | Thank you |
| I want | I would like |
| Can you...? | Could you please...? |

---

## 📚 Esempi reali

- Informale: I want information  
- Formale: I would like some information  

---

- Informale: Can you send it?  
- Formale: Could you please send it?  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Examples:**

Informal:
> Hi, can you help me with this?

Formal:
> Dear Mr Smith,  
> Could you please help me with this?

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I want → I would like  
- Can you → Could you please  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I want ___ → I would like ___  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Trasforma:

👉 da informale → formale  

Esempio:

- I want info  
→ I would like some information  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

👉 nel formale:

- ritmo più lento  
- più chiaro  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ uso troppo diretto  
❌ mancanza di cortesia  

👉 esempio:

❌ Send me this  
✅ Could you please send me this?  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

👉 formale:

- I would like...  
- Could you please...?  

---

## 🔁 Upgrade (B2 reale)

👉 aggiungi:

- I was wondering if...  
- Would it be possible to...?  

---

### Esempi

- I was wondering if you could help me  
- Would it be possible to change the time?
