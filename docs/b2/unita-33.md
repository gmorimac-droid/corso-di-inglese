# Unità 33 – Advanced Vocabulary (Collocations)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare combinazioni naturali di parole (collocations)
- evitare traduzioni dall’italiano
- parlare in modo più fluido e naturale
- migliorare il tuo vocabolario reale

---

## 🧠 Cosa sono le collocations?

👉 parole che “stanno bene insieme”

- make a decision  
- take a break  
- do homework  

👉 NON puoi scegliere a caso

---

## ⚠️ Problema tipico

❌ traduzione dall’italiano  
❌ make a photo  
✅ take a photo  

---

## 📚 Collocations fondamentali

### Make

- make a decision  
- make a mistake  
- make money  
- make a plan  

---

### Take

- take a break  
- take a photo  
- take a risk  
- take time  

---

### Do

- do homework  
- do exercise  
- do business  
- do your best  

---

## 📚 Esempi reali

- I made a decision  
- I took a break  
- I did my homework  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Text:**

> I made an important decision yesterday.  
> Then I took a break.  
> In the evening, I did some work.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- make a decision  
- take a break  
- do homework  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I made ___  
- I took ___  
- I did ___  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6 frasi:

👉 usa collocations

Esempio:

> I made a mistake.  
> I took a break.  
> I did my homework.  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

👉 importante:

- parole unite (chunking)

- make-a-decision  
- take-a-break  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ make a photo  
✅ take a photo  

❌ do a mistake  
✅ make a mistake  

❌ make homework  
✅ do homework  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

👉 NON imparare parole singole  
👉 impara **blocchi**

---

## 🔁 Strategia efficace

👉 studia così:

❌ word = decision  
✅ chunk = make a decision
