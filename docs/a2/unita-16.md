# Unità 16 – Food and Shopping

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- ordinare cibo
- fare acquisti
- parlare di quantità (base)
- sostenere conversazioni semplici in negozio o ristorante

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Countable vs Uncountable (base)

👉 Countable (numerabili)

- a pizza  
- two apples  
- three sandwiches  

---

👉 Uncountable (non numerabili)

- water  
- coffee  
- bread  
- rice  

---

### Uso base

- I want a pizza  
- I want some water

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue (restaurant):**

A: What would you like?  
B: I would like a pizza and water.  

A: Anything else?  
B: No, thank you.

---

**Dialogue (shop):**

A: Can I help you?  
B: Yes, I want apples.  

A: How many?  
B: Two, please.

---

---

👉 Ora parla:

---

👉 Fai domande:

- How much is it?  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 5 frasi:

👉 cosa mangi o compri

Esempio:

> I buy bread.  
> I drink coffee.  
> I eat pizza.  
> I want water.  
> I buy fruit.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- water → /ˈwɔːtər/  
- coffee → /ˈkɒfi/  

👉 NON leggere come italiano

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I want a water  
✅ I want water  

❌ I want breads  
✅ I want bread  

❌ I buy a milk  
✅ I buy milk  

👉 alcuni cibi NON si contano

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- I want + food  
- I buy + food  
- I eat + food  

👉 struttura semplice → molto usata
