# Unità 12 – Present Simple vs Present Continuous

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- distinguere tra routine e azioni in corso
- usare correttamente present simple e present continuous
- evitare errori molto comuni
- parlare in modo più naturale

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Present Simple → abitudini

👉 cose che fai sempre / regolarmente

- I work every day  
- I drink coffee in the morning  
- She lives in Italy  

---

### Present Continuous → adesso

👉 cosa succede ORA

- I am working  
- I am drinking coffee  
- She is living in Milan  

---

## ⚖️ Differenza chiave

| Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|---------------|------------------|
| routine       | adesso           |
| always        | now              |
| every day     | at the moment    |

---

## 📚 Esempi chiari

- I work → lavoro (in generale)  
- I am working → sto lavorando ora  

---

- I eat pizza → mangio pizza (abitualmente)  
- I am eating pizza → sto mangiando pizza ora  

---

- She lives in Rome → vive a Roma  
- She is living in Rome → vive a Roma (temporaneamente)

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: What do you do?  
B: I work in an office.  

A: What are you doing now?  
B: I am working from home.  

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I work every day  
- I am working now  

- I study English  
- I am studying now  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I ___ every day  
- I am ___ now  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6 frasi:

👉 3 routine  
👉 3 azioni adesso  

Esempio:

> I work every day.  
> I drink coffee in the morning.  
> I study English.  
>  
> I am sitting.  
> I am reading.  
> I am learning English.

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I am working every day  
✅ I work every day  

❌ I work now  
✅ I am working now  

❌ She is work  
✅ She is working  

---

## 🧠 Pattern mentale (IMPORTANTISSIMO)

👉 chiediti sempre:

- È una routine? → Present Simple  
- È adesso? → Present Continuous  

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Scegli la forma corretta:

1. I (work / am working) now  
2. I (work / am working) every day  
3. She (eats / is eating) now  
4. They (study / are studying) at the moment
