# Unità 17 – Quantifiers (some, any, much, many)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- parlare di quantità in modo corretto
- usare *some* e *any*
- usare *much* e *many*
- evitare errori molto comuni

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Some

👉 usato in frasi positive

- I have some water  
- I want some apples  

---

### Any

👉 usato in domande e negative

- Do you have any water?  
- I don’t have any money  

---

### Esempi

- I have some coffee  
- I don’t have any coffee  
- Do you have any coffee?  

---

## ⚖️ Much vs Many

### Many → countable (numerabili)

- many apples  
- many books  

---

### Much → uncountable (non numerabili)

- much water  
- much money  

---

### Esempi

- I have many books  
- I don’t have much money  

---

## 📚 Core vocabulary

### Countable

- apples  
- books  
- chairs  
- shops  

---

### Uncountable

- water  
- coffee  
- money  
- food  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: Do you have any money?  
B: No, I don’t have any money.  

A: Do you have any apples?  
B: Yes, I have some apples.

Scrivi 5 frasi:

4. I have many ______  
5. I don’t have much ______  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- some → /sʌm/  
- any → /ˈeni/  
- many → /ˈmeni/  

👉 suoni diversi → non confondere

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I don’t have some money  
✅ I don’t have any money  

❌ I have many money  
✅ I have much money  

❌ I have much apples  
✅ I have many apples  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- positive → some  
- negative → any  
- question → any  

---

## 🔁 Schema semplice

| Tipo | Uso |
|------|-----|
| positive | some |
| negative | any |
| question | any |
| countable | many |
| uncountable | much |
