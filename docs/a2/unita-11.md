# Unità 11 – Present Continuous

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- parlare di azioni in corso (adesso)
- descrivere cosa sta succedendo
- usare il present continuous correttamente
- distinguere tra “now” e “every day”

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Forma base

👉 **Subject + to be + verb-ing**

- I am working  
- You are studying  
- She is eating  

---

### Esempi

- I am eating  
- You are working  
- He is sleeping  
- They are watching TV  

---

### Forma negativa

- I am not working  
- She is not eating  
- They are not studying  

👉 contrazioni:

- I’m not  
- She isn’t  
- They aren’t  

---

### Forma interrogativa

👉 **To be + subject + verb-ing**

- Are you working?  
- Is she eating?  
- Are they watching TV?  

---

## 📚 Core vocabulary

### Verbi comuni

- eat  
- work  
- study  
- sleep  
- watch  
- read  
- talk  

---

### Espressioni utili

- now  
- at the moment  
- right now  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: What are you doing?  
B: I’m eating.  

A: Are you working?  
B: No, I’m not. I’m relaxing.  

A: What is she doing?  
B: She is studying.

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 5 frasi su cosa stai facendo ORA:

Esempio:

> I am sitting.  
> I am reading.  
> I am studying English.  
> I am drinking coffee.  
> I am listening.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- working → /ˈwɜːrkɪŋ/  
- eating → /ˈiːtɪŋ/  

👉 finale **-ing = /ɪŋ/** (non “ingh” italiano)

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I am work  
✅ I am working  

❌ I working  
✅ I am working  

❌ She is eat  
✅ She is eating  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- I am working  
- You are working  
- He is working  

👉 struttura sempre uguale → cambia solo il verbo

---

## 🔁 Confronto base

👉 differenza chiave:

- I work → routine  
- I am working → adesso
