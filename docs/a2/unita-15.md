# Unità 15 – Comparatives and Superlatives

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- fare confronti tra due cose
- dire cosa è “più” o “meno”
- esprimere preferenze
- usare comparativi e superlativi

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Comparatives (confronto tra 2)

👉 struttura:

- adjective + **-er** + than  

---

### Esempi

- Rome is bigger than Milan  
- This book is cheaper than that one  
- English is easier than German  

---

### Aggettivi lunghi

👉 usa **more**

- more expensive  
- more interesting  
- more difficult  

---

### Esempi

- This hotel is more expensive  
- This city is more interesting  

---

## 🏆 Superlatives (il massimo)

👉 struttura:

- the + adjective + **-est**

---

### Esempi

- Rome is the biggest city  
- This is the cheapest hotel  

---

### Aggettivi lunghi

👉 usa **the most**

- the most expensive  
- the most interesting  

---

### Esempi

- This is the most beautiful place  
- It is the most expensive restaurant  

---

## 📚 Core vocabulary

### Aggettivi comuni

- big  
- small  
- fast  
- slow  
- easy  
- difficult  
- expensive  
- cheap  
- beautiful  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Text:**

> Rome is bigger than Milan.  
> It is also more beautiful.  
> This hotel is cheaper than that one.  
> This is the best restaurant in the city.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- This is bigger  
- This is smaller  
- This is more expensive  
- This is cheaper  

---

👉 Confronta:

- A is bigger than B  
- This is cheaper than that  

---

👉 Superlativi:

- This is the best  
- This is the biggest  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 5 frasi:

👉 confronta due cose

Esempio:

> Rome is bigger than Milan.  
> Pizza is better than pasta.  
> This phone is more expensive.  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- bigger → /ˈbɪɡər/  
- cheaper → /ˈtʃiːpər/  

👉 non leggere tutto come italiano

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ more bigger  
✅ bigger  

❌ the more expensive  
✅ the most expensive  

❌ more cheap  
✅ cheaper  

👉 NON usare “more” con aggettivi corti

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- short adjective → bigger  
- long adjective → more interesting  

---

## 🔁 Schema semplice

| Tipo | Forma |
|------|------|
| corto | bigger |
| lungo | more interesting |
| superlativo corto | biggest |
| superlativo lungo | most interesting |
