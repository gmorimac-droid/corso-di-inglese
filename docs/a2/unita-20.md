# Unità 20 – A2 Review

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare tutti i tempi base (present, past, future)
- sostenere una conversazione semplice ma reale
- parlare di esperienze, piani e abitudini
- combinare grammatica e vocabolario

---

## 🧠 Grammar Review

Hai studiato:

- Present Simple → routine  
- Present Continuous → adesso  
- Past Simple → passato  
- Going to / Will → futuro  
- Some / Any / Much / Many  
- Comparatives  
- Have you ever…?  

👉 Ora li usiamo insieme.

---

## 📚 Vocabulary Review

Sai parlare di:

- vita quotidiana  
- viaggi  
- cibo  
- città  
- esperienze  
- piani  

👉 Ora devi combinare tutto.

---

## 📝 TEST 1 – Grammar

Completa:

1. I ___ (work) every day  
2. I ___ (work) now  
3. Yesterday I ___ (go) to Rome  
4. I have ___ (eat) sushi  
5. I am going ___ (travel) tomorrow  
6. I will ___ (call) you  
7. I have ___ apples  
8. I don’t have ___ money  
9. This is ___ (big) than that  
10. This is the ___ (best) place  

---

## 📝 TEST 2 – Trasformazioni

1. I work → (now) ______  
2. I go → (past) ______  
3. I eat → (present perfect) ______  
4. I travel tomorrow → ______  
5. I have apples → (negative) ______  

---

## 🗣️ SPEAKING TEST (fondamentale)

👉 Parla per 2 minuti

Devi includere:

- informazioni personali  
- routine  
- cosa stai facendo ora  
- cosa hai fatto ieri  
- esperienze  
- piani futuri  

---

### Esempio

> Hi, my name is Marco.  
> I live in Italy and I work in an office.  
> Every day I wake up at 7.  
> Now I am studying English.  
> Yesterday I went to the city and I ate pizza.  
> I have been to Spain.  
> Tomorrow I am going to work and I will study.

---

## ✍️ WRITING TEST

Scrivi un testo (10–12 frasi):

Deve includere:

- routine  
- passato  
- esperienza  
- futuro  

---

## 🧠 THINK & SPEAK

Rispondi:

- What do you do every day?  
- What are you doing now?  
- What did you do yesterday?  
- Have you ever travelled?  
- What are you going to do tomorrow?  

---

## 🔊 PRONUNCIATION CHECK

Ripeti:

- I am working now  
- I worked yesterday  
- I have been to Italy  
- I will call you  

👉 fluido e naturale

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (check finale)

❌ I work now  
✅ I am working now  

❌ I have went  
✅ I have gone  

❌ I will to go  
✅ I will go  

❌ I don’t have some money  
✅ I don’t have any money  

---

## 🚀 FINAL TASK (livello reale)

Simula una conversazione:

👉 incontri qualcuno

Devi:

- presentarti  
- fare 3 domande  
- parlare del passato  
- parlare del futuro
