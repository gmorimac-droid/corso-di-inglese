# Unità 27 – Modals (must, should, have to)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- dare consigli
- esprimere obblighi e necessità
- parlare di regole
- usare *must*, *should* e *have to* correttamente

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Should → consigli

👉 per dire cosa è una buona idea

- You should study more  
- You should sleep early  

---

### Must → obbligo forte / regole

👉 forte, spesso personale

- You must stop  
- You must study  

---

### Have to → obbligo reale

👉 regole esterne / necessità

- I have to work  
- I have to wake up early  

---

## ⚖️ Differenza chiave

| Modal | Uso |
|------|-----|
| should | consiglio |
| must | obbligo forte |
| have to | obbligo reale |

---

## 📚 Esempi reali

- You should study English every day  
- You must wear a seatbelt  
- I have to work tomorrow  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: I’m tired.  
B: You should sleep more.  

A: I have an exam.  
B: You must study.  

A: I have a job.  
B: I have to work every day.

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6 frasi:

👉 2 consigli  
👉 2 obblighi  
👉 2 necessità  

Esempio:

> You should study more.  
> You should eat healthy food.  
> I must finish this work.  
> You must stop here.  
> I have to wake up early.  
> I have to go to work.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- should → /ʃʊd/  
- must → /mʌst/  

👉 suoni brevi e veloci

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I must to go  
✅ I must go  

❌ I have to go  
✅ (corretto)  

❌ You should to study  
✅ You should study  

👉 dopo modali → verbo base (no “to”)

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- Subject + modal + verb  

👉 sempre senza “to”

---

## 🔁 Negativo

- You shouldn’t smoke  
- You mustn’t do that  
- I don’t have to work today  

---

## 📚 Differenza negativa (importante)

- mustn’t → proibizione  
- don’t have to → non necessario  

---

### Esempi

- You mustn’t smoke → vietato  
- You don’t have to work → non serve  

---
