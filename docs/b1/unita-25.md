# Unità 25 – Giving Opinions

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- esprimere opinioni in modo naturale
- essere d’accordo o in disaccordo
- sostenere una conversazione reale
- usare frasi più fluide e naturali

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Esprimere opinioni

👉 strutture base:

- I think...  
- I believe...  
- In my opinion...  

---

### Esempi

- I think this film is good  
- I believe English is important  
- In my opinion, this is the best option  

---

## 📚 Espressioni utili

### Agreement (accordo)

- I agree  
- You’re right  
- I think so  

---

### Disagreement (disaccordo)

- I don’t agree  
- I don’t think so  
- I think you’re wrong  

---

### Opinioni più naturali

- I think it’s interesting  
- I don’t think it’s a good idea  
- It’s better to...  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: I think this restaurant is very good.  
B: Yes, I agree.  

A: I think it’s expensive.  
B: I don’t agree. I think it’s ok.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I think it’s good  
- I don’t think it’s good  
- I agree  
- I don’t agree  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I think ___  
- I don’t think ___  

---

👉 Interazione:

- Do you agree?  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 5 frasi:

👉 esprimi opinioni

Esempio:

> I think English is important.  
> I think this city is beautiful.  
> I don’t think this is a good idea.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- I think → /aɪ θɪŋk/  
👉 suono TH (molto importante)

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I think yes  
✅ I think so  

❌ I agree with you (sempre)  
👉 meglio variare:  
✅ You’re right  

❌ I am agree  
✅ I agree  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- I think...  
- I don’t think...  

👉 struttura base per parlare

---

## 🔁 Espansione (B1 reale)

👉 aggiungi:

- because  

---

### Esempi

- I think it’s good because it’s cheap  
- I don’t think it’s a good idea because it’s expensive  

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Completa:

1. I ___ it’s good  
2. I don’t ___ it’s good  
3. I ___ with you  
4. I don’t ___  

---

## 🔄 Trasforma

Completa:

1. It is good → I think ______  
2. It is bad → I don’t think ______  

---

## 🧠 Think & Speak

Parla:

👉 esprimi opinioni su:

- food  
- travel  
- work  
- English  

---

## 🚀 Mini task

Parla per 2 minuti:

👉 dai 3 opinioni + spiegazione  

---

## 💬 Extra practice

Rispondi:

- Do you think English is important?  
- Do you think travelling is useful?  
- Do you agree?  

---

## 🎯 Challenge

Crea:

- 3 opinioni  
- 2 disaccordi  
- usa *because*  

---

## 🧩 Bonus (B1+)

Usa:

- I think...  
- I believe...  
- In my opinion...  

👉 per variare
