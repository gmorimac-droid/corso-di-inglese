# Unità 28 – Emails and Messages

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- scrivere email semplici
- distinguere tra formale e informale
- scrivere messaggi naturali
- comunicare in contesti reali (lavoro, amici)

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

👉 qui usiamo:

- Present Simple  
- Present Perfect  
- Future  

👉 ma soprattutto:
- struttura e tono

---

## 📚 Tipi di comunicazione

### Informale (amici)

- Hi!  
- How are you?  
- See you soon  

---

### Formale (lavoro)

- Dear Mr Smith,  
- I hope you are well  
- Best regards  

---

## 📧 Struttura email semplice

### 1. Apertura

- Hi John, (informale)  
- Dear Mr Smith, (formale)  

---

### 2. Corpo

- I am writing to...  
- I would like to...  

---

### 3. Chiusura

- See you soon (informale)  
- Best regards (formale)  

---

## 📚 Espressioni utili

### Informale

- How are you?  
- I hope you are well  
- Let’s meet  
- See you soon  

---

### Formale

- I am writing to inform you...  
- I would like to ask...  
- Thank you for your time  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Email (informale):**

> Hi John,  
> How are you?  
> I am writing to invite you to dinner.  
> Let me know.  
> See you soon!  

---

**Email (formale):**

> Dear Mr Smith,  
> I hope you are well.  
> I am writing to ask about the meeting.  
> Thank you for your time.  
> Best regards,  
> Marco  

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

👉 anche se è scritto → leggilo ad alta voce:

- I am writing to...  
- I would like to...  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi:

### 1 email informale

👉 a un amico

---

### 1 email formale

👉 a un collega / azienda  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- writing → /ˈraɪtɪŋ/  
- regards → /rɪˈɡɑːrdz/  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ Dear friend  
✅ Hi / Hello  

❌ I wait your answer  
✅ I look forward to your reply  

❌ I write you  
✅ I am writing to you  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

### Email formale:

- I am writing to...  
- I would like to...  

---

### Email informale:

- Hi!  
- How are you?  
- Let’s...  

---

## 🔁 Differenza chiave

| Informale | Formale |
|----------|--------|
| Hi | Dear |
| See you | Best regards |
| Let’s meet | I would like to meet |
