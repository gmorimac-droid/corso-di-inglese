# Unità 21 – Present Perfect (Real Use)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare il present perfect in modo naturale
- collegare passato e presente
- parlare di esperienze, risultati e cambiamenti
- evitare errori tipici italiani

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Struttura

👉 **Subject + have/has + past participle**

- I have worked  
- She has studied  
- They have travelled  

---

## 🔑 Uso reale (IMPORTANTISSIMO)

### 1. Esperienze

- I have been to Spain  
- I have eaten sushi  

👉 (non dici quando)

---

### 2. Risultati (adesso)

- I have finished my work  
- I have lost my keys  

👉 effetto nel presente

---

### 3. Cambiamenti

- I have learned English  
- She has improved a lot  

---

## ⚖️ Present Perfect vs Past Simple

👉 differenza fondamentale:

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|----------------|------------|
| esperienza | quando |
| no tempo preciso | tempo preciso |

---

### Esempi

- I have been to Rome (esperienza)  
- I went to Rome last year (quando)  

---

## 📚 Core vocabulary

### Past participles comuni

- go → gone  
- eat → eaten  
- see → seen  
- do → done  
- make → made  
- take → taken  

---

### Espressioni utili

- already  
- just  
- yet  

---

### Esempi

- I have already finished  
- I have just eaten  
- I haven’t finished yet  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: Have you finished your work?  
B: Yes, I have just finished.  

A: Have you seen that film?  
B: No, I haven’t seen it yet.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I have finished  
- I have just eaten  
- I haven’t done it yet  

---

👉 Ora parla:

- I have ___  
- I haven’t ___ yet  

---

👉 Fai domande:

- Have you finished?  
- Have you seen it?  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6 frasi:

👉 usa:

- already  
- just  
- yet  

Esempio:

> I have already finished my work.  
> I have just eaten lunch.  
> I haven’t watched that film yet.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- have → /həv/ (debole)  
- finished → /ˈfɪnɪʃt/  

👉 parlato naturale:

- I’ve finished  
- She’s done  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I have went  
✅ I have gone  

❌ I have done yesterday  
✅ I did it yesterday  

❌ I am finished  
✅ I have finished  

---

## 🧠 Pattern mentale (CHIAVE B1)

👉 chiediti:

- È importante quando? → Past Simple  
- È importante il risultato? → Present Perfect
