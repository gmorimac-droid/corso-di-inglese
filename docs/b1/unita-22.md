# Unità 22 – Present Perfect vs Past Simple

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare correttamente present perfect e past simple
- capire quando usare uno o l’altro
- evitare gli errori più comuni degli italiani
- parlare in modo naturale

---

## 🧠 Differenza chiave (IMPORTANTISSIMA)

👉 chiediti sempre:

- WHEN? (quando?) → Past Simple  
- RESULT / EXPERIENCE? → Present Perfect  

---

## ⚖️ Confronto diretto

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|----------------|------------|
| esperienza | momento preciso |
| senza tempo | con tempo |
| risultato | evento finito |

---

## 📚 Esempi chiari

### Esperienza

- I have been to Rome  
👉 (non dici quando)

---

### Tempo preciso

- I went to Rome last year  
👉 (quando)

---

### Risultato

- I have finished my work  
👉 (importante adesso)

---

### Evento finito

- I finished my work yesterday  

---

## 🔁 Confronto reale

- I have eaten → ho mangiato (non importa quando)  
- I ate at 8 → ho mangiato alle 8  

---

- I have seen that film → esperienza  
- I saw that film yesterday → quando  

---

## 📚 Parole chiave

### Present Perfect

- already  
- just  
- yet  
- ever  
- never  

---

### Past Simple

- yesterday  
- last week  
- in 2020  
- two days ago  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: Have you finished your work?  
B: Yes, I have finished.  

A: When did you finish?  
B: I finished two hours ago.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I have finished  
- I finished yesterday  

---

👉 Ora prova:

- I have ___  
- I ___ yesterday  

---

👉 Fai domande:

- Have you finished?  
- When did you finish?  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6 frasi:

👉 3 present perfect  
👉 3 past simple  

Esempio:

> I have eaten sushi.  
> I have finished my work.  
> I have seen that film.  
>  
> I ate sushi yesterday.  
> I finished my work at 6.  
> I saw that film last week.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- I’ve finished  
- I finished  

👉 differenza sottile ma importante

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I have done yesterday  
✅ I did it yesterday  

❌ I went many times  
✅ I have gone many times  

❌ I have been in Rome in 2020  
✅ I went to Rome in 2020  

---

## 🧠 Regola d’oro

👉 MAI usare Present Perfect con:

- yesterday  
- last week  
- in 2020  

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Scegli la forma corretta:

1. I (have eaten / ate) yesterday  
2. I (have seen / saw) that film  
3. I (have finished / finished) two hours ago  
4. I (have been / went) to Spain
