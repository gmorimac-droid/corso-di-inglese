# Unità 26 – Work and Study

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- parlare del tuo lavoro o dei tuoi studi
- descrivere responsabilità e attività
- sostenere una conversazione base professionale
- rispondere a domande tipiche (anche da colloquio)

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

👉 usiamo:

- Present Simple → lavoro abituale  
- Present Perfect → esperienza  
- Future → piani  

---

### Esempi

- I work in an office  
- I have worked in this company for 2 years  
- I am going to change job  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue (job interview):**

A: What do you do?  
B: I work in a company.  

A: What are your responsibilities?  
B: I manage projects and help clients.  

A: How long have you worked there?  
B: I have worked there for two years.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti:

- I work in an office  
- I study at university  
- I manage projects  
- I help people  

---

👉 Ora parla:

- I work as ___  
- I study ___  
- I help ___  

---

👉 Rispondi:

- What do you do?  
- Where do you work?  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 6–8 frasi:

👉 parla del tuo lavoro o studio

Esempio:

> I work in an office.  
> I help clients.  
> I have worked here for two years.  
> I study English in my free time.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione:

- work → /wɜːrk/  
- study → /ˈstʌdi/  

👉 non leggere “study” come italiano

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I do a work  
✅ I work  

❌ I make a job  
✅ I have a job  

❌ I study to university  
✅ I study at university  

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

- I work as ___  
- I work in ___  
- I am responsible for ___  

---

## 🔁 Espansione (B1 reale)

👉 usa:

- I am responsible for...  
- I deal with...  

---

### Esempi

- I am responsible for projects  
- I deal with clients  

---

## ❓ Domande tipiche

- What do you do?  
- Where do you work?  
- What do you study?  
- What are your responsibilities?  

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Completa:

1. I ___ in a company  
2. I ___ English  
3. I have ___ (work) here for 2 years  
4. I am responsible ___ projects  

---

## 🔄 Trasforma

Completa:

1. I work → I have ______ (experience)  
2. I study → I am ______
