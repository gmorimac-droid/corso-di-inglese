## 🧠 Grammar Review

Hai studiato:

- Present Simple → routine  
- Present Continuous → adesso / futuro  
- Past Simple → eventi passati  
- Present Perfect → esperienze / risultati  
- Future (will / going to / present continuous)  
- First Conditional (if...)  
- Modals (should, must, have to)  
- Phrasal verbs (base)  

👉 Ora li usi insieme.

## 📝 TEST 1 – Grammar

Completa:

1. I ___ (work) every day  
2. I ___ (work) now  
3. Yesterday I ___ (go) out  
4. I have ___ (eat) sushi  
5. I ___ (go) to Rome last year  
6. I am going ___ (travel) tomorrow  
7. I will ___ (call) you  
8. If I ___ (study), I will pass  
9. You should ___ (study)  
10. I ___ up at 7 (phrasal verb)  

---

## 📝 TEST 2 – Mixed Practice

Scegli la forma corretta:

1. I (have eaten / ate) yesterday  
2. I (am working / work) now  
3. If it (rains / will rain), I will stay home  
4. I (have been / went) to Spain  
5. I (will help / am going to help) you (decisione ora)  

---

## 🗣️ SPEAKING TEST (CRUCIALE)

👉 Parla per 3 minuti

Devi includere:

- informazioni personali  
- routine  
- cosa stai facendo ora  
- cosa hai fatto recentemente  
- esperienze  
- opinioni  
- piani futuri  

---

### Esempio

> Hi, my name is Marco.  
> I live in Italy and I work in an office.  
> Every day I wake up at 7 and I go to work.  
> Now I am studying English.  
> Yesterday I went out with friends.  
> I have been to Spain and I really liked it.  
> I think travelling is very important because you learn a lot.  
> Tomorrow I am going to work and I will study in the evening.

---

## ✍️ WRITING TEST

Scrivi un testo (12–15 frasi):

Deve includere:

- routine  
- passato  
- esperienza  
- opinione  
- futuro  

---

## 🧠 THINK & SPEAK

Rispondi:

- What do you do every day?  
- What did you do yesterday?  
- Have you ever travelled?  
- What do you think about learning English?  
- What will you do tomorrow?  

---

## 🔊 PRONUNCIATION CHECK

Ripeti:

- I’ve finished my work  
- I went out yesterday  
- I’m going to travel  
- If I study, I’ll pass  

👉 fluido, senza pause

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (check finale)

❌ I have done yesterday  
✅ I did it yesterday  

❌ If I will study  
✅ If I study  

❌ I will to go  
✅ I will go  

❌ I search my keys  
✅ I look for my keys
