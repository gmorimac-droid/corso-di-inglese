# Unità 49 – Nuance & Precision (Choosing the Right Word)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- scegliere la parola più precisa
- capire differenze sottili tra parole simili
- esprimerti con maggiore accuratezza
- evitare inglese “generico”

---

## 🧠 Il salto da B2 a C1

👉 B2:

- usi parole corrette  

👉 C1:

- usi parole precise  

---

👉 non è:

✔️ parola giusta  

👉 ma:

✔️ parola migliore  

---

# 🧩 Cos’è la “nuance”?

👉 piccola differenza di significato  

---

### Esempio

- big  
- large  
- huge  

👉 tutte corrette  
👉 ma NON identiche  

---

---

# 🔁 Parole simili, significati diversi

---

## 🔥 Say vs Tell

- say → dire  
- tell → dire a qualcuno  

---

### Esempi

- He said something  
- He told me something  

---

---

## 🔥 Job vs Work

- job → lavoro specifico  
- work → attività  

---

### Esempi

- I have a job  
- I have a lot of work  

---

---

## 🔥 Make vs Do

- make → creare  
- do → attività  

---

### Esempi

- make a decision  
- do homework  

---

---

## 🔥 See vs Watch vs Look

| Verbo | Uso |
|------|-----|
| see | percezione |
| watch | attenzione |
| look | azione |

---

### Esempi

- I see him  
- I watch TV  
- I look at the screen  

---

---

# 🧠 Livello C1: Precisione

---

## 🔧 Good vs Great vs Excellent

👉 tutti positivi  
👉 ma livello diverso  

---

- good → base  
- great → forte  
- excellent → molto forte  

---

---

## 🔧 Problem vs Issue

- problem → problema diretto  
- issue → più formale / neutro  

---

---

## 🔧 Idea vs Point

- idea → pensiero  
- point → argomento  

---

---

# 🔁 Confronto reale

❌ base:

- It is very very good  

---

✔️ C1:

- It is excellent  
- It is outstanding  

---

---

# 🧠 Register (fondamentale)

👉 scegliere parole in base al contesto  

---

## Informale

- get  
- stuff  
- things  

---

## Formale

- obtain  
- items  
- elements  

---

---

### Esempio

❌ I got some stuff  
✔️ I obtained several items  

---

---

# 🧠 Evitare parole “deboli”

👉 parole da ridurre:

- very  
- things  
- stuff  

---

👉 sostituire con parole precise  

---

### Esempi

- very big → huge  
- very small → tiny  
- very good → excellent  

---

---

# 🎧 Reading

> The situation was not just difficult; it was extremely complex. Several key factors contributed to the issue, and it required careful analysis to reach a conclusion.

---

# 🗣️ Speaking

👉 sostituisci parole generiche  

---

### Esempio

❌ It was very good  
✔️ It was extremely effective

---

# ✍️ Writing

👉 evita:

# ⚠️ Errori comuni (C1)

| Errore | Corretto |
|--------|----------|
| very very big | huge |
| do a decision | make a decision |
| say me | tell me |
