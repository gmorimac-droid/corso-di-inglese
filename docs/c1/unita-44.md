# Unità 44 – Passive Voice (Advanced & Natural Use)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare il passive in modo naturale (non scolastico)
- scegliere tra active e passive in base al contesto
- usare il passive per essere più formale e professionale
- costruire frasi più fluide e sofisticate

---

## 🧠 Il salto da B2 a C1

👉 B2:

- sai costruire il passive  

👉 C1:

- sai quando usarlo  

---

👉 non è più:

✔️ “so fare il passive”  

👉 ma:

✔️ “ha senso usarlo qui?”  

---

# 🧩 Cos’è davvero il Passive?

👉 sposta il focus:

- da chi fa l’azione  
→ all’azione stessa  

---

## ⚖️ Confronto

- Active: Someone wrote this report  
- Passive: This report was written  

---

👉 nel passive:

❌ non importa chi  
✔️ importa il risultato  

---

---

# 🧠 Struttura base (rapida)

👉 be + past participle  

---

| Tempo | Esempio |
|------|---------|
| Present | is done |
| Past | was done |
| Present Perfect | has been done |
| Future | will be done |

---

---

# 🔥 Uso reale (C1)

---

## 1. Quando NON interessa chi

- The report was finished  
- The project was completed  

---

## 2. Quando è ovvio chi

- The suspect was arrested  
👉 (police)

---

## 3. Linguaggio formale

- The decision was made  
- The results were analysed  

---

---

# 🧠 Passive nei tempi avanzati

---

## Present Perfect Passive

- The work has been completed  

---

## Past Perfect Passive

- The project had been finished before we arrived  

---

## Future Passive

- The work will be done tomorrow  

---

---

# 🧩 Passive con modals

👉 struttura:

modal + be + past participle  

---

### Esempi

- The work must be done  
- This can be improved  
- The problem should be solved  

---

---

# 🔁 Passive vs Active (C1 scelta)

---

## Active

- We finished the project  

👉 più diretto  

---

## Passive

- The project was finished  

👉 più neutro / formale  

---

---

# 🧠 Uso avanzato: Impersonal Passive

👉 molto usato in inglese formale

---

### Esempi

- It is said that...  
- It is believed that...  
- It is known that...  

---

👉 esempio:

> It is believed that this method is effective  

---

---

# 🧠 Passive con “get”

👉 più informale / parlato

---

### Esempi

- He got fired  
- She got promoted  

---

👉 differenza:

- was fired → neutro  
- got fired → più personale  

---

---

# 🎧 Reading

> The project was completed ahead of schedule, and the results have been widely praised. It is believed that this approach will be used in future projects.

---

---

# 🗣️ Speaking

👉 usa passive per:

- descrivere processi  
- parlare in modo formale  

---

### Esempio

> The product is developed in Italy and is exported worldwide.

---

---

# ✍️ Writing

Scrivi un testo (10–12 frasi):

👉 usa:

- almeno 4 passive  
- almeno 2 tempi diversi  

---

---

# ⚠️ Errori comuni (C1)

| Errore | Corretto |
|--------|----------|
| The work done | The work was done |
| The project is finish | The project is finished |
| It is say that | It is said that |

---

---

# 🧠 Pattern avanzati

👉 strutture chiave:

- has been done  
- was completed  
- will be finished  

---

---

# 🔁 Upgrade (C1 reale)

👉 combina active + passive

---

### Esempio

> We completed the project, and the results were published last week.

---

---

# 🧠 Strategia mentale

👉 chiediti:

- importa chi fa l’azione?  

👉 se NO → usa passive
