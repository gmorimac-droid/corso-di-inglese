# 🧩 Cos’è un phrasal verb?

👉 verbo + particella  

---

### Esempi base

- look for → cercare  
- give up → arrendersi  
- take off → decollare / togliere  

---

👉 il significato NON è sempre logico  

---

---

# ❌ Errore tipico

👉 tradurre parola per parola

---

| Phrasal verb | Significato |
|-------------|------------|
| give up | smettere |
| run into | incontrare per caso |
| take over | prendere controllo |

---

---

# 🔥 Livello C1: significato figurato

---

## 🧠 Esempi avanzati

---

### Bring up

- He brought up an interesting point  
👉 (menzionare)

---

### Turn down

- She turned down the offer  
👉 (rifiutare)

---

### Carry on

- Carry on with your work  
👉 (continuare)

---

---

# 🧠 Multiple meanings (fondamentale)

👉 un phrasal verb = più significati

---

### Take off

- The plane took off  
👉 decollare  

- The business took off  
👉 avere successo  

---

👉 C1 = capire dal contesto  

---

---

# 🔁 Separable vs Inseparable

---

## Separable

- turn off the light  
- turn the light off  

---

## Inseparable

- look after someone  
❌ look someone after  

---

---

# 🧠 Phrasal verbs comuni (C1)

---

## 🔧 Azioni e decisioni

- carry out → eseguire  
- come up with → trovare un’idea  
- go through → affrontare  

---

## 🔗 Relazioni

- get along with → andare d’accordo  
- fall out → litigare  

---

## 🔄 Lavoro e progresso

- move forward → avanzare  
- step down → dimettersi  
- take on → assumere / accettare  

---

---

# 🔁 Confronto reale

❌ base:

- I continued my work  

---

✔️ naturale:

- I carried on with my work  

---

---

# 🧠 Strategia C1

👉 NON memorizzare liste  

👉 impara:

✔️ uso reale
