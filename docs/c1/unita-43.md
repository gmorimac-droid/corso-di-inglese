# Unità 43 – Modal Verbs (Speculation & Deduction)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- fare deduzioni logiche
- esprimere certezza e probabilità
- parlare di situazioni presenti e passate in modo sofisticato
- usare i modal verbs come un madrelingua

---

## 🧠 Il salto da B2 a C1

👉 B2:

- can, should, must = regole  

👉 C1:

- modal verbs = **interpretazione della realtà**

---

👉 non descrivi più cosa succede  
👉 ma **cosa pensi che succeda**

---

# 🧠 Deduction (presente)

---

## 🔴 Must (certezza alta)

👉 sono sicuro

- He must be tired  
- She must be at work  

---

## 🟡 Might / May / Could (possibilità)

👉 non sono sicuro

- He might be at home  
- She could be working  

---

## ⚫ Can’t (impossibilità)

👉 sono sicuro che NON

- He can’t be at home  
- This can’t be true  

---

---

## 📊 Confronto

| Modal | Significato |
|------|-------------|
| must | 90% sicuro |
| might / may / could | possibile |
| can’t | impossibile |

---

---

# 🧠 Deduction (passato)

👉 qui diventa C1 vero

---

## 🔴 Must have + past participle

👉 quasi certo

- He must have left early  
- She must have forgotten  

---

## 🟡 Might / May / Could have

👉 possibile

- He might have missed the train  
- She could have gone home  

---

## ⚫ Can’t have

👉 impossibile

- He can’t have done that  
- She can’t have seen it  

---

---

## 🧩 Esempio reale

> The lights are off. He must have gone out.

---

---

# 🧠 Differenza chiave

| Tempo | Uso |
|------|-----|
| must be | presente |
| must have been | passato |

---

---

# 🔁 Confronto reale

- He must be tired  
👉 adesso  

- He must have been tired  
👉 nel passato  

---

---

# 🧠 Speculation vs Reality

👉 non descrivi fatti  
👉 descrivi interpretazioni  

---

## Esempio

- She is late → fatto  
- She must be stuck in traffic → interpretazione  

---

---

# 🎧 Reading

> He didn’t answer the phone. He must have been busy, or he might have been in a meeting.

---

---

# 🗣️ Speaking

👉 usa:

- must  
- might  
- can’t  

---

### Esempio

> He’s not here. He must be working, but he might be at home.

---

---

# ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 10 frasi:

👉 includi:

- 3 deduzioni presenti

---

# ⚠️ Errori comuni (C1)

| Errore | Corretto |
|--------|----------|
| He must to be tired | He must be tired |
| He must has gone | He must have gone |
| He can’t to go | He can’t go |

---

---

# 🧠 Pattern avanzati

👉 struttura chiave:

- modal + base  
- modal + have + past participle  

---

---

# 🔁 Upgrade (C1 reale)

👉 combina deduzioni:

---

### Esempio

> He must have been tired because he didn’t answer, but he might have been working.

---

---

# 🧠 Strategia mentale

👉 chiediti:

- cosa vedo?  
- cosa penso?  
- quanto sono sicuro?
