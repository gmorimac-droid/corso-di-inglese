## 🧠 Il salto da B2 a C1

👉 B2:

- uso corretto dei tempi  

👉 C1:

- uso preciso dei tempi  

---

👉 non è più:

✔️ “è giusto o sbagliato”  

👉 ma:

✔️ “qual è più naturale?”  
✔️ “qual è più preciso?”  

---

## ⚖️ Present Perfect vs Past Simple (Nuance)

### Base (già conosciuta)

- I went → tempo preciso  
- I have gone → esperienza / risultato  

---

### Livello C1 (sfumatura)

👉 confronto:

- I lived in London for 2 years  
👉 esperienza finita  

- I have lived in London for 2 years  
👉 esperienza collegata al presente  

---

### Differenza reale

| Frase | Significato |
|------|-------------|
| I lived there | non vivo più lì |
| I have lived there | potrebbe essere ancora vero |

---

---

## 🧠 Present Perfect Continuous vs Simple

👉 confronto avanzato:

- I have worked all day  
- I have been working all day  

---

### Differenza

| Forma | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Present Perfect | risultato |
| Present Perfect Continuous | durata / processo |

---

### Esempi

- I have written 5 emails  
👉 risultato  

- I have been writing emails all morning  
👉 attività continua  

---

---

## 🔁 Past Continuous vs Past Simple

👉 non solo “azione in corso”

---

### Esempio avanzato

- I was working when he called  
👉 contesto  

- I worked when he called  
👉 sequenza (meno naturale)  

---

### Uso C1

👉 Past Continuous = background  

---

### Esempio

> I was walking through the city when I saw something strange.

---

---

## 🔮 Future Nuance (C1)

👉 confronto:

- I will do it  
- I’m going to do it  
- I’m doing it  

---

### Differenze reali

| Forma | Uso |
|------|-----|
| will | decisione / promessa |
| going to | intenzione |
| present continuous | piano organizzato |

---

### Livello C1

👉 scegliere in base al contesto:

- I’ll call you (decisione ora)  
- I’m going to call you (intenzione)  
- I’m calling you at 8 (programma)

## 🧠 Time Perspective (C1 Skill)

👉 pensa sempre:

- quando succede?  
- è finito?  
- è ancora rilevante?  

---

## 🧩 Esempio avanzato

> I have been working here for 3 years, and I really enjoy it. Before that, I worked in a different company.

👉 perfetto mix tempi

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (livello avanzato)

| Errore | Corretto |
|--------|----------|
| I am living here since 2 years | I have been living here for 2 years |
| I have worked yesterday | I worked yesterday |
| I work here since 2020 | I have worked here since 2020 |

---

---

## 🧠 Pattern avanzati

👉 combinare tempi:

- I have been working here and I really enjoy it  
- I worked there before I moved here
