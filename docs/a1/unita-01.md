# Unità 01 – Greetings and Introductions

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- salutare
- presentarti
- dire il tuo nome e la tua provenienza
- fare domande base

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Il verbo "to be"

| Subject | Verb |
|--------|------|
| I      | am   |
| You    | are  |
| He/She | is   |
| We     | are  |
| They   | are  |

### Esempi

- I am Marco  
- You are a student  
- She is from Italy  

---

## 📚 Core vocabulary

### Greetings

- Hello  
- Hi  
- Good morning  
- Good afternoon  
- Good evening  

### Introductions

- My name is...  
- I am...  
- Nice to meet you  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: Hello!  
B: Hi!  
A: My name is John. What’s your name?  
B: I’m Anna. Nice to meet you.  
A: Nice to meet you too.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti ad alta voce:

- Hello, my name is ___
- I am from ___
- Nice to meet you

👉 Poi rispondi:

- What’s your name?
- Where are you from?

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 3 frasi:

1. My name is ______  
2. I am from ______  
3. I am a ______  

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione a:

- "Hello" → /həˈloʊ/
- "Name" → /neɪm/

👉 Non dire: *“ello”* → la **H si pronuncia**

---

## ✅ Review & Practice

Completa:

1. I ___ Marco  
2. She ___ from Spain  
3. My name ___ Anna
