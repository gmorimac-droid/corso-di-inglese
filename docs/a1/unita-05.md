# Unità 05 – Present Simple

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- parlare della tua vita quotidiana
- descrivere abitudini
- usare il present simple in modo corretto
- costruire frasi semplici ma reali

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Forma base

👉 **Subject + verb**

- I work  
- You live  
- They study  

---

### Terza persona (he / she / it)

👉 aggiungi **-s**

- He works  
- She lives  
- It eats  

---

### Esempi

- I work in an office  
- You live in Milan  
- He works in a hospital  
- She studies English  

---

## 📚 Core vocabulary

### Verbi comuni

- work  
- live  
- eat  
- study  
- like  
- drink  
- go  
- play  

---

### Espressioni utili

- every day  
- in the morning  
- at night  
- on Monday  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: What do you do?  
B: I work in an office.  

A: Where do you live?  
B: I live in Milan.  

A: What does she do?  
B: She works in a hospital.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione alla **-s finale**

- works → /wɜːrks/  
- lives → /lɪvz/  

👉 Non ignorare la *-s*

❌ He work  
✅ He works  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ He work  
✅ He works  

❌ She live in Italy  
✅ She lives in Italy  

❌ I am work  
✅ I work  

👉 NON usare *to be* con verbi normali

---

## 🧠 Pattern fondamentale

Impara questo schema:

- I work  
- You work  
- He works  

👉 Questo pattern è la base di tutto l’inglese.
