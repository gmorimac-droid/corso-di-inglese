# Unità 03 – Personal Information

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- chiedere e dare informazioni personali
- fare domande con *what*, *where*, *how old*
- parlare di nome, età, lavoro e provenienza
- sostenere una mini conversazione reale

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Domande con WH-words

Struttura:

👉 **WH-word + verb + subject**

---

### What (cosa)

- What is your name?  
- What is your job?  

👉 Forma contratta:

- What’s your name?  
- What’s your job?  

---

### Where (dove)

- Where are you from?  

👉 Attenzione:

❌ Where you are from  
✅ Where are you from  

---

### How old (età)

- How old are you?  

👉 Risposta:

- I am 25 years old  
- I’m 25  

---

## 📚 Core vocabulary

### Personal information

- name  
- surname / last name  
- age  
- job  
- country  
- city  

---

### Jobs (review + nuovi)

- student  
- teacher  
- doctor  
- engineer  
- manager  

---

## 🎧 Listening / Reading

**Dialogue:**

A: Hi! What’s your name?  
B: My name is Luca.  
A: Where are you from?  
B: I’m from Italy.  
A: How old are you?  
B: I’m 28. And you?  
A: I’m 30. I’m from Spain.

---

## 🗣️ Speaking

Ripeti ad alta voce:

- What’s your name?  
- Where are you from?  
- How old are you?  

---

👉 Rispondi:

- My name is ___  
- I’m from ___  
- I’m ___ years old  

---

👉 Ora fai un mini dialogo (anche da solo):

- What’s your name?  
- Where are you from?  
- How old are you?  

---

## ✍️ Writing

Scrivi un breve testo (3–5 frasi):

Esempio:

> My name is Marco.  
> I am from Italy.  
> I am 25 years old.  
> I am a student.

---

👉 Ora scrivi il tuo.

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Attenzione alle contrazioni:

- What is → **What’s**  
- Where are → **Where’re** (più difficile, spesso ridotto nel parlato)

---

### Focus su "years old"

- years → /jɪərz/  
👉 non dire: *“iers”*

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I have 30 years  
✅ I am 30 years old  

❌ What is your name? (sempre lento e rigido)  
✅ What’s your name? (naturale)

❌ Where you are from?  
✅ Where are you from?  

---
