# Unità 02 – Verb "To Be"

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare correttamente il verbo *to be*
- fare frasi affermative, negative e domande
- dare informazioni semplici su persone
- rispondere a domande base

---

## 🧠 Grammar in context

### Forma affermativa

| Subject | Verb |
|--------|------|
| I      | am   |
| You    | are  |
| He     | is   |
| She    | is   |
| It     | is   |
| We     | are  |
| They   | are  |

### Esempi

- I am a student  
- You are my friend  
- She is from Italy  
- They are teachers  

---

### Forma negativa

Struttura:

👉 **Subject + verb + NOT**

- I am not (I’m not)  
- You are not (You aren’t)  
- He is not (He isn’t)  

### Esempi

- I am not a teacher  
- She is not from Spain  
- They are not at home  

---

### Forma interrogativa

Struttura:

👉 **Verb + subject**

- Am I...?  
- Are you...?  
- Is he...?  

### Esempi

- Are you a student?  
- Is she Italian?  
- Are they friends?  

---

### Risposte brevi

- Yes, I am / No, I’m not  
- Yes, he is / No, he isn’t  
- Yes, they are / No, they aren’t

---

## 🔊 Pronunciation

Contrazioni molto importanti:

- I am → **I’m**  
- You are → **You’re**  
- He is → **He’s**  
- She is → **She’s**  
- They are → **They’re**

👉 Parla in modo naturale:

❌ I am Marco  
✅ I’m Marco  

---

## ⚠️ Errori comuni (italiani)

❌ I have 25 years  
✅ I am 25 years old  

❌ I am agree  
✅ I agree  

👉 In inglese *to be* è usato in modo diverso rispetto all’italiano.
