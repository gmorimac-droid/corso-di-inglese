# Unità 58 – Storytelling (Advanced Narrative Skills)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- raccontare storie in modo fluido e coinvolgente
- organizzare eventi in modo chiaro e naturale
- usare tempi verbali in modo efficace
- mantenere l’attenzione dell’ascoltatore

---

## 🧠 Il salto da C1 a C2

👉 C1:

- descrivi eventi  

👉 C2:

- racconti storie  

---

👉 non è più:

✔️ cosa è successo  

👉 ma:

✔️ come lo racconti  

---

---

# 🧩 Cos’è lo storytelling?

👉 raccontare una sequenza di eventi  

con:

- struttura  
- ritmo  
- emozione  

---

---

# 🧠 Struttura di una storia (C2)

---

## 1. Inizio (setup)

👉 introduci contesto  

- It all started when...  
- A few years ago...  

---

## 2. Sviluppo

👉 cosa succede  

- Then...  
- After that...  

---

## 3. Momento chiave

👉 qualcosa cambia  

- Suddenly...  
- Unexpectedly...  

---

## 4. Conclusione

👉 risultato  

- In the end...  
- Eventually...  

---

---

# 📚 Esempio completo

> A few years ago, I decided to travel alone. At first, everything was exciting, but then I got lost in a city I didn’t know. Suddenly, I realized I had no internet connection. In the end, I managed to find my way back, and it became one of the most memorable experiences of my life.

# 🧠 Tempi verbali nello storytelling

---

## 🔧 Past Simple

👉 eventi principali  

- I went  
- I saw  

---

## 🔧 Past Continuous

👉 contesto  

- I was walking  
- I was thinking  

---

## 🔧 Past Perfect

👉 eventi precedenti  

- I had already left  

---

---

# 🔁 Confronto reale

❌ base:

- I went there. It was nice.  

---

✔️ C2:

- I went there, and at first everything seemed perfect, but then something unexpected happened.

---

---

# 🧠 Discourse markers nello storytelling

---

- then  
- suddenly  
- eventually  
- in the end  

---

👉 creano ritmo

---

# ⚠️ Errori comuni (C2)

| Errore | Soluzione |
|--------|-----------|
| frasi isolate | collegare |
| poca struttura | seguire schema |
| no emozione | aggiungere dettagli |

---

---

# 🧠 Pattern avanzati

👉 strutture utili:

- I didn’t realize that...
