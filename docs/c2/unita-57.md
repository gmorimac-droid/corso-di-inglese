# 🧩 Cos’è un’argomentazione?

👉 una struttura:

1. opinione  
2. motivazione  
3. esempio  
4. conclusione  

---

---

# 🧠 Struttura base (C2)

---

## 1. Opinione

- I believe that...  
- In my opinion...  

---

## 2. Argomento

- One of the main reasons is...  
- This is because...  

---

## 3. Esempio

- For example...  
- A clear example is...  

---

## 4. Conclusione

- Therefore...  
- For this reason...  

---

---

# 📚 Esempio completo

> I believe that working remotely has many advantages. One of the main reasons is that it allows people to manage their time more effectively. For example, employees can avoid commuting and focus more on their tasks. Therefore, remote work can increase productivity.

# 🔁 Agreement & Disagreement

---

## ✔️ Agreement

- I completely agree  
- That’s a good point  
- I see your point  

---

## ❗ Partial agreement

- I agree to some extent  
- That may be true, but...  

---

## ❌ Disagreement

- I don’t agree  
- I’m not convinced  
- I see it differently  

---

---

# 🧠 Softening (fondamentale)

👉 rendere opinioni meno aggressive  

---

## Esempi

- I think → I would argue that  
- I disagree → I’m not entirely convinced  

---

---

# 🧠 Linking ideas (C2)

---

## Aggiunta

- furthermore  
- in addition  

---

## Contrasto

- however  
- on the other hand  

---

## Conclusione

- therefore  
- consequently  

---

---
