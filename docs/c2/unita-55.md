# Unità 55 – Discourse Markers (Natural Conversation Flow)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- usare discourse markers in modo naturale
- rendere il tuo parlato fluido e spontaneo
- gestire conversazioni reali
- sembrare più simile a un madrelingua

---

## 🧠 Il salto da C1 a C2

👉 C1:

- inglese corretto  

👉 C2:

- inglese naturale  

---

👉 differenza chiave:

❌ frasi perfette ma rigide  
✔️ flusso naturale  

---

---

# 🧩 Cos’è un discourse marker?

👉 parole che:

- collegano idee  
- gestiscono il discorso  
- riempiono pause naturali  

---

### Esempi

- actually  
- basically  
- well  
- you know  
- I mean  

---

👉 NON cambiano il significato  
👉 cambiano la naturalezza  

---

---

# 🔥 Discourse markers comuni

---

## 🧠 Per organizzare

- first of all  
- anyway  
- in the end  

---

## 🔄 Per correggere / chiarire

- I mean  
- actually  
- in fact  

---

## 💬 Per conversazione

- well  
- you know  
- kind of  
- sort of  

---

---

# 🧠 Esempi reali

---

## Without markers (B2)

- I didn’t like it because it was difficult  

---

## With markers (C2)

- Well, I didn’t really like it because it was kind of difficult  

---

👉 più naturale  

---

---

# 🧠 Funzioni principali

---

## 🔧 Prendere tempo

- well...  
- you know...  

---

## 🔧 Correggere

- actually...  
- I mean...  

---

## 🔧 Attenuare

- kind of  
- sort of  

---

---

# 🔁 Confronto reale

❌ rigido:

- I disagree  

---

✔️ naturale:

- Well, I’m not sure I agree  

---

---

# 🧠 Livello C2: spontaneità

👉 i madrelingua:

- non parlano perfetto  
- parlano naturale  

---

👉 usano discourse markers continuamente  

---

---

# 🎧 Reading

> Well, I think it’s actually more complicated than it seems. I mean, there are a lot of factors to consider, and, you know, it’s not always easy to decide.

---

---

# 🗣️ Speaking

👉 usa:

- well  
- actually  
- I mean  
- you know  

---

### Esempio

> Well, I think it’s actually quite interesting, but, you know, it can be a bit difficult at times.

---

---

# ✍️ Writing

👉 uso limitato (più parlato)

Scrivi 5–8 frasi:

👉 stile semi-informale  

---

---

# ⚠️ Errori comuni (C2)

| Errore | Soluzione |
|--------|-----------|
| non usarli | integrarli |
| usarli troppo | equilibrio |
| usarli a caso | usare con funzione |

---

---

# 🧠 Pattern avanzati

👉 combinazioni naturali:

- Well, I think...  
- Actually, I don’t think so  
- I mean, it depends  

---

---

# 🔁 Upgrade (C2 reale)

👉 integra markers nel discorso

---

### Esempio

> Well, I think it’s actually a good idea, but, you know, it depends on the situation.

---

---
