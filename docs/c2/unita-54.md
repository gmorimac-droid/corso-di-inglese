# Unità 54 – Ellipsis & Substitution (Natural Flow)

---

## 🎯 Obiettivo

Alla fine di questa unità sarai in grado di:

- evitare ripetizioni inutili
- rendere il tuo inglese più fluido e naturale
- usare ellipsis e substitution correttamente
- parlare e scrivere in modo più “native-like”

---

## 🧠 Il salto da C1 a C2

👉 C1:

- inglese corretto e preciso  

👉 C2:

- inglese naturale e fluido  

---

👉 differenza chiave:

❌ dire tutto  
✔️ dire solo ciò che serve  

---

---

# 🧩 Cos’è l’ellipsis?

👉 eliminare parole già chiare  

---

## Esempio

- I can play the guitar, and she can play the guitar  

---

✔️ naturale:

- I can play the guitar, and she can too  

---

---

# 🧩 Cos’è la substitution?

👉 sostituire parole con altre più brevi  

---

## Esempio

- I need a pen. Do you have a pen?  

---

✔️ naturale:

- I need a pen. Do you have one?  

---

---

# 🔥 Tipi di ellipsis

---

## 🔧 Verbi ripetuti

- I work more than he does  
- She speaks better than I do  

---

---

## 🔧 Auxiliaries

- I have finished, and she has too  

---

---

## 🔧 Risposte brevi

- Are you ready?  
→ Yes, I am  

---

---

# 🔥 Tipi di substitution

---

## 🔁 One / Ones

- I like this shirt, but I prefer that one  

---

## 🔁 Do / Does / Did

- I don’t like it, but she does  

---

## 🔁 So / Not

- I think so  
- I hope not  

---

---

# 🧠 Perché è importante?

👉 senza ellipsis:

❌ ripetitivo  
❌ innaturale  

---

👉 con ellipsis:

✔️ fluido  
✔️ naturale  
✔️ efficiente  

---

---

# 🔁 Confronto reale

❌ base:

- I like this movie, and she likes this movie too  

---

✔️ C2:

- I like this movie, and she does too  

---

---

# 🧠 Livello C2: naturalezza

👉 i madrelingua:

- eliminano  
- riducono  
- sostituiscono  

---

👉 non parlano “completo”  

---

---

# 🎧 Reading

> I thought the first option was better, but she didn’t. In fact, she preferred the second one.

---

---

# 🗣️ Speaking

👉 usa:

- one  
- do / does  
- too / so  

---

### Esempio

> I enjoyed the first part, but she didn’t.

---

---

# ✍️ Writing

Scrivi 10 frasi:

👉 usando:

- ellipsis  
- substitution  

---

---

# ⚠️ Errori comuni (C2)

| Errore | Corretto |
|--------|----------|
| ripetere tutto | ridurre |
| non usare one | usarlo |
| frasi troppo lunghe | semplificare |

---

---

# 🧠 Pattern avanzati

👉 strutture utili:

- I do, too  
- I don’t either  
- So do I  

---

---

# 🔁 Upgrade (C2 reale)

👉 combinare ellipsis + connectors

---

### Esempio

> I liked the first option, but she didn’t, and I think she prefers the second one.

---

---

# 🧠 Strategia mentale

👉 chiediti:

- posso dire meno?  

---

👉 se sì:

✔️ riduci
